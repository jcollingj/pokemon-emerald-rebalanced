# Beedrill

Poison Setup Sweeper. Poisons the opponent, then Merciless guarantees crits on every hit. Pin Missile with Loaded Dice = 4-5 guaranteed crits in one turn.

## Stats

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced |

| Atk | 90 | **115** |
| Def | 40 | 40 |
| SpA | 45 | 45 |
| SpD | 80 | 80 |
| Spe | 75 | **105** |
| **BST** | **395** | **460** |

## The Combo

1. Poison Powder → opponent poisoned
2. Pin Missile (Loaded Dice) → 4-5 guaranteed crits → ~225-281 effective STAB power
3. Most things die instantly

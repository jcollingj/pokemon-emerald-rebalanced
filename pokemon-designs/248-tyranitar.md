# Tyranitar

## Build Identity
Sand Bulky Attacker — Sand Stream summons sandstorm on entry, boosting Rock-type SpDef by 50% (100 to effective 150). Sets Stealth Rock, attacks with 134 Atk dual STAB, and Dragon Dances late-game to sweep.

## Ability
Sand Stream (summons sandstorm for 5 turns on entry)

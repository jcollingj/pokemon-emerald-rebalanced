# Carvanha

Glass cannon pre-evo that bites hard with Strong Jaw-boosted STAB moves. Bite and Crunch (Dark STAB) come online early, with Aqua Jet for priority Water STAB. 90 Atk at base form is unusually high -- Carvanha hits like an evolved Pokemon but has 20/20 defenses. Speed shifted from SpA to guarantee outspeeding early-game threats. Appears on Team Aqua Grunt's team at L9 in Petalburg Woods as a showcase.

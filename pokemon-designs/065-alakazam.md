# Alakazam

## Build Identity
Alakazam is a nuclear special sweeper that uses Magic Guard to run Life Orb with zero recoil — 135 SpA × 1.3 = **175 effective SpA** with no downside. 120 Speed outspeeds most threats. Dies to any physical hit, but nothing passive can touch it.

## Ability
- **Ability 1:** Magic Guard (no indirect damage — Life Orb recoil, hazards, weather, status chip all ignored)
- **Ability 2:** Inner Focus (flinch immunity)
- **Hidden:** Synchronize

## Core Strategy

1. Switch in — Magic Guard ignores hazards completely
2. Life Orb gives +30% damage with zero recoil
3. Use Calm Mind or Nasty Plot if there's a free turn
4. Rotate coverage: Psychic (STAB), Shadow Ball (Ghost), Dazzling Gleam (Dragon), Focus Blast (Steel/Dark via TM)
5. Recover buys more turns — only direct hits matter

**The weakness:** 55 HP / 45 Def means any fast physical attacker or priority move (Mach Punch, Bullet Punch, Extreme Speed) OHKOs Alakazam. Pure glass cannon — boom or bust.

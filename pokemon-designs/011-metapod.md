# Metapod

## Build Identity
The hard cocoon. Shell Armor prevents critical hits. Buffed Def and SpD make it a genuinely survivable transition Pokemon rather than a death sentence. Evolves at L10.

## Ability
- **Ability 1: Shell Armor** — No critical hits. The cocoon is indestructible. Thematic and practical.
- **Ability 2: Shed Skin** — 33% chance to heal status each turn. Can Harden through burns and poison.

# Butterfree

## Build Identity
Compound Eyes turns Sleep Powder into a 97.5% accurate move. Butterfree evolves at L10 with Sleep Powder already in kit. By L14 it has Quiver Dance. The loop is immediate: sleep → setup → sweep. Feels complete the moment it evolves.

## Ability
- **Ability 1: Compound Eyes** — Core identity. Sleep Powder 97.5%, Stun Spore 97.5%, Air Slash 100%, Hurricane 91%.
- **Ability 2: Tinted Lens** — Alt build: skip sleep entirely. Hurricane is unresisted. Choice Specs + Tinted Lens + Hurricane.

**The L10/12/14 priority:** Butterfree is immediately usable. Sleep Powder at evolution (L10), an attacking move to follow up (L12), and the setup move (L14). All three pieces available before the first major checkpoint.

## Core Strategy

### The Setup Loop
1. **Sleep Powder** (L10) — 97.5% accurate. Enemy sleeps.
2. **Quiver Dance** (L14) — free +1 SpA / SpD / Speed.
3. **Bug Buzz / Hurricane** — sweep. After one Quiver Dance: 180 SpA, 157 Speed.

### Ideal Items
1. **Leftovers** — sustains free setup turns. 1-2 Quiver Dances while they sleep.
2. **Big Root** — Dream Eater (TM): sleep → drain HP. Permanent recovery.
3. **Choice Specs** — Tinted Lens alt: Hurricane hits everything neutrally at 180 effective SpA.

## Niche vs Other Bugs
- **Beedrill:** Poisons then crits (Merciless). Physical combo.
- **Butterfree:** Sleeps then sweeps (Compound Eyes + Quiver Dance). Special setup.
- Completely distinct roles — both can coexist on the same team.

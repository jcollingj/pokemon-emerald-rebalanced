# Sharpedo

STAB power assassin that uses Strong Jaw to amplify Water and Dark biting moves to extreme levels. The centerpiece is Fishious Rend -- 85 BP Water bite that doubles when moving first. With Strong Jaw (1.5x) + STAB (1.5x) + speed double (2x), Fishious Rend hits 382 effective BP off 120 Atk. Crunch provides a reliable 180 effective Dark nuke. Jaw Lock traps opponents to guarantee a follow-up Fishious Rend. Differentiates from Raticate (also Strong Jaw) by concentrating power into 2 STAB types rather than spreading across 6 coverage fangs. 70/40/40 bulk means Sharpedo dies fast, but the damage it deals in 1-2 turns is among the highest in the game.

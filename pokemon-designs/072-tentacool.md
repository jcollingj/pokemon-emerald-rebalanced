# Tentacool

Water/Poison trapping poison tank. Poison Heal + Toxic Orb heals 1/8 HP per turn and blocks all other status. Gets Whirlpool at level 8 (buffed: traps + lowers Atk), trapping opponents early. Acid Spray at 12 shreds SpDef on trapped targets. Evolves at 30 into Tentacruel (vanilla level). Available via Old Rod on Route 103.

See [design doc](../../docs/game-design/tentacool.md) for full details.

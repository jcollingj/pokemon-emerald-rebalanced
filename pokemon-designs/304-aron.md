# Aron

**Type:** Steel / Rock
**Ability:** Heavy Metal / Sturdy / Rock Head (hidden)
**Evolution:** → Lairon at L32 → Aggron at L42

## Build Identity

Aron is the early-game setup piece of the armored titan line. Caught in Rusturf Tunnel at L10-12, it immediately has Metal Claw for Steel STAB. Iron Defense at L16 starts the Def-stacking combo that pays off fully in Aggron.

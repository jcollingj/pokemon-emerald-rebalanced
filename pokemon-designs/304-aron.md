# Aron

## Build Identity

Aron is the early form of Aggron, the Recoil-Free Physical Wall. Rock Head removes all recoil from Take Down and eventually Head Smash — every recoil move in the kit is free.

## Ability

**Rock Head** — Prevents all recoil damage from moves like Take Down, Double-Edge, and Head Smash.

# Nincada

## Build Identity
Nincada is a Speed Boost ramp harasser that hides behind 90 Def, dropping opponent accuracy with Sand Attack and Mud Slap while building Speed stacks — becoming surprisingly fast and annoying before splitting into Ninjask and Shedinja at L20.

## Ability
**Speed Boost** — Speed rises +1 each turn in battle. Consistent with Ninjask's identity. By turn 3-4, Nincada is outrunning most early-game opponents while they're accuracy-crippled.

- Alt: Compound Eyes (+30% accuracy — makes Fury Swipes ~94% accurate per hit)
- Hidden: Sand Veil (evasion in sand — niche)

## Ideal Items

1. **Lum Berry** — Prevents sleep/paralysis that would stall Speed Boost ramp
2. **Eviolite** — Bumps 90 Def to 135 effective; with Speed Boost building, Nincada becomes hard to KO
3. **Sitrus Berry** — Sustains through early chip while Speed stacks accumulate

## Core Strategy
Switch in, use Harden if needed, then stack Sand Attack + Mud Slap. After 2-3 turns: Speed Boost has activated twice, opponent is accuracy-crippled. Nincada is now outspeeding most opponents in its level range. Dig threatens with real 80 BP Ground STAB. High 90 Def lets it survive hits while setting up.

## Team Synergy
Primarily a pre-evolution — use it until L20 and appreciate it more once you see what Ninjask and Shedinja do with the same Speed Boost foundation.

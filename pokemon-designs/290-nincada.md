# Nincada

**Build Identity:** Nincada is an early-game Bug/Ground attacker that uses Compound Eyes to make Fury Swipes reliable and Mud Slap's accuracy drops more consistent — a precision grub that invests into a powerful split evolution at L20.

## Ability: Compound Eyes

Accuracy ×1.3. Fury Swipes (80%) → 104% effective — never misses. Mud Slap and Leech Life also benefit. Thematically perfect for a Pokemon with literal compound eyes.

## Ideal Items

1. **Eviolite** — +50% to both defenses while unevolved. Pairs well with high base Def.
2. **Lum Berry** — Prevents early-game status (sleep/paralyze/poison)
3. **Scope Lens** — Crit rate boost; Fury Swipes multi-hit generates more crit rolls

## Core Strategy

Use Mud Slap to drop accuracy, then Fury Swipes to chunk with reliable multi-hit. Leech Life for sustain. Harden if you need to outlast a threat. At L20 you get the biggest payoff in the game: two Pokemon from one evolution.

# Nincada

**Build Identity:** Nincada is an early-game Bug/Ground pre-evolution that uses Compound Eyes to make accuracy-dependent moves more reliable, bridging to its unique split evolution at L16.

## Evolution
Evolves at **Level 16** → Ninjask (+ Shedinja with an empty party slot and Poke Ball)

## Notes
Compound Eyes makes Fury Swipes 91% accurate (up from 70%). Mud Slap's accuracy drop compounds against opponents who already have lowered accuracy. Available on Route 116 at L6-7.

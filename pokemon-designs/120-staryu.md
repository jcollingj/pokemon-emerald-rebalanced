# Staryu

**Build Identity:** Staryu is a fast special attacker that uses Natural Cure to pivot freely. At 85 Speed and 75 SpA, it's capable of threatening things before evolution — Water Pulse + Confusion gives it real dual-STAB offense, and Recover at L16 keeps it healthy.

## Ability

**Natural Cure** — status heals on switch-out. Even as Staryu, this enables guilt-free pivoting when opponents try to status it down.

## Ideal Items

1. **Eviolite** — Boosts Def/SpD by 50% on a pre-evolution. Makes Staryu surprisingly durable (85 Speed + Recover + Eviolite = actually hard to kill for its stage).
2. **Oran Berry / Sitrus Berry** — Simple healing item if evolving soon.
3. **Twisted Spoon / Mystic Water** — Boosts Psychic or Water by 20% for extra punch pre-evolution.

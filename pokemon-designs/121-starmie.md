# Starmie

**Build Identity:** Starmie is the fastest, broadest special attacker in the early-to-mid game. 115 SpA + 115 Speed with Natural Cure makes it a hit-and-run sweeper that can't be permanently neutered by status. It clicks coverage, pivots out to clear status, and comes back clean.

## Ability

**Natural Cure** — status heals on switch-out.

The sweeper identity is defined by this ability: Starmie attacks aggressively, and when opponents try to slow it with Toxic/Paralysis/Burn, it simply switches out to reset. No Lum Berry slot required. No worrying about lasting status damage.

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Specs** — 115 SpA x 1.5 = 172 effective SpA. Surf at ~202 effective BP. Being locked in is acceptable because Natural Cure triggers on switch-out and Starmie re-enters with a new move selection.
2. **Life Orb** — 1.3x SpA, flexible moveset. Run Surf / Psychic / Power Gem / Recover without locking in. Better vs. varied opponents.
3. **Eject Button** — Ejects on hit, triggering Natural Cure. Synergizes with the hit-and-run identity — you're rewarded for taking damage.

## Core Strategy

**Life Orb Flexible:**

**Cosmic Power Setup:**
- Surf / Psychic / Cosmic Power / Recover

- Strategy: Set up bulk, Natural Cure clears status on forced pivots

## Niche

Starmie is the fastest special attacker in the Water lineup — distinct from Swampert (defensive/rain), Tentacruel (trapping/tank), and Pelipper (rain setter). Its niche: pure speed-based special offense that can't be pinned down with status.

# Starmie

## Build Identity
The fastest Water type in the game at 115 Speed, with symmetric 115 SpA. Illuminate turns its natural beam learnset into 5-type coverage where almost every move is boosted. **Every attack is a laser.** Psychic and Surf are deliberately NOT boosted — forcing interesting moveset choices.

## Items
1. **Choice Specs** — 115 SpA × 1.5 × 1.3 = ~224 effective on beams. Nuclear.
2. **Life Orb** — Power without move lock, covers Surf/Psychic too
3. **Expert Belt** — Reward for 5-type coverage hitting super effectively

## Core Strategy
1. Come in on favorable matchup — 115 Speed means you usually move first
2. Pick the right beam for the type — almost nothing resists three of your beam types
3. Charge Beam in safe spots to escalate SpA and snowball
4. Tension: Ice Beam (117 effective) > Surf (90) for neutral hits — Water STAB is often not the play

## Notes
- Niche: **Beam Mage** — distinct from all other Water types in game
- Not a Pulse Mage (that's Blastoise) — different boosted moves, different coverage profile
- Psybeam often outperforms Psychic with Illuminate at 115 SpA — a satisfying design discovery
- Illuminate fantasy: every attack is a different colored laser from the gem

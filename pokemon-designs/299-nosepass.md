# Nosepass

## Build Identity
Nosepass is the pre-evolution of a sand fortress that uses Sand Force to boost Rock/Ground/Steel moves by 1.3x in sandstorm, while sandstorm's SpDef boost to Rock types makes it deceptively tanky on the special side.

## Ability
**Sand Force** — 1.3x damage to Rock/Ground/Steel moves in sandstorm. Every offensive move benefits.

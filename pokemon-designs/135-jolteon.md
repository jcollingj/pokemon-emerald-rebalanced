# Jolteon

**Build Identity:** The fastest Eeveelution — a pivot sweeper that uses Volt Switch to gain momentum, Thunderbolt for reliable STAB, and Thunder for maximum power. 130 Speed makes it one of the fastest Pokemon in Segment 1-3.

## Ability
**Volt Absorb** — Immune to Electric moves, restores 1/4 HP. Switch into Electric attacks freely.

## Core Strategy
Lead with Thunder Wave → Volt Switch to pivot into favorable matchup → Thunderbolt for reliable damage → Thunder for max power late game. Volt Absorb means you can switch into opposing Electric users for free healing.

# Minun

**Build Identity:** Dedicated doubles bodyguard that exists to protect Plusle. High bulk + Follow Me + Friend Guard (-25% damage to allies) make Plusle nearly unkillable. Minun doesn't deal damage — it enables Plusle to deal damage safely.

**Ability 1:** Friend Guard — Reduces damage to allies by 25% in doubles. Stacks with Minun's bulk.
**Ability 2:** Minus — 1.5× SpA when Plusle is on field. Backup offensive option.
**Hidden:** Unaware — Ignores opponent's stat changes. Laughs at Swords Dance/Calm Mind setups.

**Key Level-Up Moves:**
- L8: Nuzzle (paralysis)
- L12: Follow Me (redirects all single-target attacks to Minun)
- L16: Helping Hand (+50% to Plusle's next move)
- L21: Charm (−2 Atk)
- L27: Fake Tears (−2 SpD, softens targets for Plusle)
- L33: Wish (sustain cycling)
- L38: Light Screen (team SpD halved)
- L42: Encore
- L48: Heal Bell (team status cleanse)

**The Fantasy:** Minun says "hit me instead" with Follow Me. Plusle says thanks and fires 115 SpA Thunderbolts from behind the bodyguard. Player needs a spread move (Earthquake, Surf) or must break through 95/75/95 bulk to reach Plusle. The Wish cycle means killing Minun slowly doesn't work — it heals back.

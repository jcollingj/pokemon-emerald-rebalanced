# Plusle

**Build Identity:** Slow artillery cannon that hides behind Minun's Follow Me and fires devastating Electric attacks. Speed gutted from 95 → 50; SpA boosted from 85 → 115.

**Ability 1:** Plus — 1.5× SpA when Minun is on the field. 115 SpA × 1.5 = 172 effective SpA, free.
**Ability 2:** Solar Power — 1.5× SpA in sunshine, -1/8 HP/turn. Glass cannon mode for solo play.
**Hidden:** Technician — Powers up moves ≤60 BP (Nuzzle, Charge Beam, ThunderShock).

**Stats:**

**The Fantasy:** Moves last every turn, but Minun always covers with Follow Me. Nasty Plot to +2, then Discharge both opponents simultaneously off 172+ effective SpA. The player can't touch Plusle without a spread move.

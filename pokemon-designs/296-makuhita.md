# Makuhita

## Build Identity
Makuhita is an Iron Fist puncher that evolves quickly (L16). It's your first Fighting type and immediately puts Mach Punch priority on the table at L8. Short-lived but capable — a bridge to Hariyama.

## Ability
**Iron Fist** — 1.2x damage on punching moves. Already active on Mach Punch and Fire Punch before evolution.

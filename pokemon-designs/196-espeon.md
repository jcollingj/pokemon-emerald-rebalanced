# Espeon

**Build Identity:** A special sweeper with Magic Bounce as its primary ability, reflecting hazards and status moves back at opponents. Calm Mind + Stored Power creates a devastating late-game snowball.

## Ability
**Magic Bounce** — Reflects status moves, hazards, and stat-lowering moves back at the user. Espeon is the anti-hazard answer for your team.

## Core Strategy
Switch in on Stealth Rock/Toxic/status users — Magic Bounce reflects everything back. Then Calm Mind to stack SpA/SpD. Psychic for immediate damage, Stored Power for late-game snowball (base 20 + 20 per boost stage). Dazzling Gleam covers the Dark weakness.

# Probopass

## Build Identity
Probopass is an immovable sand fortress that uses Sand Force to boost Rock/Ground/Steel offense while sandstorm's 1.5x SpDef boost makes its 150 SpDef effectively 225 — nearly unbreakable on the special side. Body Press turns 145 Def into Fighting-type offense.

## Ability
**Sand Force** — 1.3x damage to Rock/Ground/Steel moves in sandstorm. Every offensive STAB benefits.

# Shedinja

## Build Identity
Shedinja is a 1-HP Wonder Guard assassin that switches into immune matchups and strikes first with Shadow Sneak priority, backed by Destiny Bond as the ultimate 1-HP trade threat.

## Ability
**Wonder Guard** — Only super effective moves can hit Shedinja. THIS IS SHEDINJA. Cannot be changed — Wonder Guard is the entire identity.

No alternate ability. Wonder Guard only.

## Stat Changes

**1 HP is permanent.** But the offense needs to be threatening. +30 Atk makes Shadow Sneak actually hurt. +40 Spe (to 80) means Shedinja often moves first in the matchups it walls, reducing reliance on priority.

## Ideal Items

1. **Lum Berry** — Prevents status (Sleep, Paralysis, Burn) that bypass Wonder Guard indirectly. The most important protection item.
2. **Jaboca Berry** — If a contact move lands (super effective), damages the attacker. Shedinja faints but takes them with it.
3. **Eject Button** — Emergency exit if Wonder Guard fails; survive to fight another day.

## Core Strategy

1. Scout the opponent's team. Identify something Shedinja is immune to (anything not Ghost, Fire, Flying, or Rock).
2. Switch Shedinja in on a confirmed immune matchup.
3. Shadow Sneak priority strikes before the opponent reacts.
4. If they bring in a Ghost/Fire/Flying/Rock threat: use Destiny Bond — they KO Shedinja, they die too.

The 80 Speed (up from 40) means Shedinja often doesn't even need Shadow Sneak's priority — it naturally outspeeds many threats in the matchups it walls.

**Weakness:** Entry hazards, weather damage (Sandstorm, Hail), and burn all bypass Wonder Guard. Keep Shedinja away from those situations.

## Team Synergy
Shedinja covers teammates weak to Ghost/Psychic/Normal. Don't pair it with Ninjask — they come from the same Nincada and filling two team slots with one evolution line is already strong enough. Pick one.

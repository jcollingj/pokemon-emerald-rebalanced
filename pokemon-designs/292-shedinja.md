# Shedinja

**Build Identity:** Shedinja is a Wonder Guard pivot striker that switches into immune matchups and threatens priority damage before opponents can react. Its 1 HP is sacred — it can never be changed. The game is: predict the right window, switch in, Shadow Sneak, or threaten Destiny Bond.

## Ability: Wonder Guard

Only super-effective moves hit Shedinja. 10 of 18 types do zero damage. Kept as-is — Wonder Guard IS Shedinja. The HP is also sacred at 1 and must never change.

**Immune to:** Normal, Fighting, Bug, Poison, Ground, Rock, Steel, Water, Grass, Electric
**Weak to:** Fire, Flying, Rock, Ghost, Dark — plus passive damage (sandstorm, burn, poison, Stealth Rocks)

## Ideal Items

1. **Lum Berry** — Prevents status (burn/poison = instant death). Most important item.
2. **Safety Goggles** — Blocks spore/powder moves that end it immediately.
3. **Choice Band** — If running pure offense (Shadow Sneak + Leech Life). 120 Atk × 1.5 = 180 effective. Priority Shadow Sneak hits surprisingly hard.

## Core Strategy

**Priority Window:** Opponent uses Normal/Fighting/Poison/etc → switch Shedinja in → Shadow Sneak for priority damage before they can react.

**Destiny Bond Threat:** Switch in on a dangerous matchup → Destiny Bond → opponent must choose: attack and die, or switch. Free turn either way.

**Will-O-Wisp Wall:** Switch into a Normal-type physical attacker (immune) → burn them → chip with Shadow Claw while they're weakened.

**Hard counters:** Stealth Rocks (instant death), passive weather damage, burn/poison status. Always bring a Rapid Spin user.

## Team Synergy

- **Rapid Spin user** — Mandatory. Stealth Rocks instakills Shedinja.
- **Ninjask (parent line)** — Ninjask Baton Passes Speed to Shedinja for a faster Shadow Sneak; or Shedinja pivots in when Ninjask needs safety.
- **Prediction partners** — Teammates who read incoming Normal/Fighting moves to safely bring Shedinja in.

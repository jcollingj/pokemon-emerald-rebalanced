# Shedinja

**Build Identity:** Shedinja is a 1-HP Ghost/Bug Pokemon with Wonder Guard (only SE moves damage it). It switches into immunities and uses Shadow Sneak priority to strike first before opponents can switch to a counter — then threatens Destiny Bond as a final trade.

## Ideal Items
1. **Focus Sash** — survive one OHKO, enabling a guaranteed second action (attack or Destiny Bond)
2. **Choice Band** — 120 Atk + Band = 180 effective; hit hard before getting countered
3. **Scope Lens** — pair with Shadow Claw's high crit rate

## Core Strategy
Switch Shedinja in on a move it's immune to (Normal, Water, Grass, Fighting, Ice, Electric, Psychic, Dragon, Fairy, Poison, Bug). Use Shadow Sneak (priority) to hit before opponent can switch to a counter. If they stay and can't touch it, keep attacking. If cornered, Destiny Bond trades Shedinja for the opposing Pokemon.

**Warning:** Stealth Rock is Shedinja's #1 counter — always check for hazards before switching in.

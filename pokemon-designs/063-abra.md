# Abra

## Build Identity
Pure Psychic glass cannon. Found at Route 116 (L7). Evolves to Kadabra at L16.

Magic Guard is the keystone — no indirect damage enables Life Orb with no recoil on Alakazam.

## Stats (BST: 310)
HP 25 | Atk 20 | Def 15 | SpA 105 | SpD 55 | Spe 90

## Abilities
- **Magic Guard** (primary): Immune to indirect damage — Life Orb recoil, hazards, weather, status chip
- **Inner Focus** (alt): Flinch immunity
- **Synchronize** (hidden): Reflects status conditions

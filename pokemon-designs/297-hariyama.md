## Iron Fist Math

- Mach Punch: 40 × 1.2 = **48 eff, +1 priority**
- Fire/Ice/Thunder Punch: 75 × 1.2 = **90 eff** each
- Drain Punch: 75 × 1.2 = **90 eff + drain**
- Focus Punch: 150 × 1.2 = **180 eff**

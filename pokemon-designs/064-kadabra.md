# Kadabra

## Build Identity
High-speed Psychic attacker with 120 SpA and 105 Speed. Magic Guard + Recover makes it surprisingly durable for a glass cannon — nothing passive can kill it, so only direct hits matter.

## Ability
- **Ability 1:** Magic Guard (no indirect damage)
- **Ability 2:** Inner Focus (flinch immunity)
- **Hidden:** Synchronize

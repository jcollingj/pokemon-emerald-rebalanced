# Kadabra

## Build Identity
Mid-stage of the Life Orb Assassin line. Gains full coverage suite (Shadow Ball, Dazzling Gleam, Psychic, Energy Ball, Focus Blast) and Recover. Evolves to Alakazam at L36.

## Stats (BST: 400)
HP 40 | Atk 35 | Def 30 | SpA 120 | SpD 70 | Spe 105

## Abilities
- **Magic Guard** (primary): Immune to indirect damage
- **Inner Focus** (alt): Flinch immunity
- **Synchronize** (hidden): Reflects status conditions

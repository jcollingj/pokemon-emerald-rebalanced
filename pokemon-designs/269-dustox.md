# Dustox

Prankster utility wall that grinds opponents with priority status. 75/90/90 bulk with priority Toxic, Sticky Web, Whirlwind, Light Screen, and Toxic Spikes. Speed dumped to 40 (Prankster handles priority). Venoshock (130 BP on poisoned targets) + Bug Buzz for damage when needed. Controls the pace of battle rather than winning through raw power.

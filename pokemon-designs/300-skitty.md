# Skitty

Early-game form of the Normalize line. Evolves into Delcatty at Level 16.

## Stats (BST: 355)

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced |
|------|---------|------------|
| HP | 50 | 55 |
| ATK | 45 | 50 |
| DEF | 45 | 50 |
| SPA | 35 | 65 |
| SPD | 35 | 55 |
| SPE | 50 | 80 |
| **BST** | **260** | **355** |

## Abilities
- **Normalize** (primary) — All moves become Normal type + 1.2× boost. With STAB = 1.8× effective on everything.
- Cute Charm (secondary)
- Wonder Skin (hidden)

# Goldeen

Custom ability **Lancer** boosts piercing/horn moves by 1.3x. Goldeen gets Horn Attack at L8 and Waterfall at L12 as early synergy moves. Horn Leech at L27 provides sustain. Evolves at L33 (vanilla). Stats buffed slightly to 335 BST (+15). See full design: `docs/game-design/goldeen.md`.

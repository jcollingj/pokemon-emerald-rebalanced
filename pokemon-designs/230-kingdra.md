# Kingdra

Water/Dragon critical hit sniper. Reworked Sniper grants passive +1 crit stage + 2.25x crit damage, so Snipe Shot crits 50% of the time with no setup. Add Scope Lens for guaranteed Snipe Shot crits, or Focus Energy for guaranteed crits on everything. Draco Meteor spam works because crits ignore the user's negative stat drops. 115 SpA + 90 Speed + only Dragon/Fairy weaknesses = terrifying sweeper that's dangerous from turn 1.

**Stats:** 75/75/90/115/95/90 (BST 540, redistributed from vanilla's 95-across-the-board)
**Ability:** Sniper (passive +1 crit stage + 2.25x crit damage) / Swift Swim / Damp (HA)

**Core play:** Snipe Shot immediately — 50% crit for 270 effective BP. Scope Lens pushes to 100%. Focus Energy guarantees crits on the full moveset including Draco Meteor (439 effective BP).

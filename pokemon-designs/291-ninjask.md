# Ninjask

## Build Identity
Ninjask is a Speed Boost sweeper and Baton Passer with 160 base Speed — the fastest Pokemon in the game — that either sweeps with Swords Dance + X-Scissor or passes accumulated boosts to a heavy hitter.

## Ability
**Speed Boost** — Speed rises +1 each turn. THIS IS NINJASK. 160 base Speed + Speed Boost = untouchable after turn 1. No other ability competes.

- Alt: Infiltrator (bypasses Reflect/Light Screen/Substitute — useful against defensive teams)
- Hidden: Pressure (forces 2 PP per opponent move — interesting in long battles)

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Band** — 100 Atk × 1.5 = 150 effective Atk off 160 Speed. Sweep mode.
2. **Focus Sash** — Guarantees survival turn 1 for Baton Pass strategy; get Swords Dance + 2 Speed Boosts before passing
3. **Salac Berry** — Triggers at low HP for additional Speed boost; layers with Speed Boost for absurd late-game Speed

## Core Strategy

**Sweeper mode:** Speed Boost gives +1 on turn 1. Swords Dance + 1 Speed Boost means +2 Atk and 240 effective Speed. X-Scissor off 100 Atk + Swords Dance hits hard enough to KO most non-resist opponents.

**Baton Pass mode:** Focus Sash, switch in, stack Speed Boosts (+ maybe Swords Dance), Baton Pass to a slow powerhouse. Transforms a 70-speed teammate into an unstoppable threat.

## Team Synergy
Pairs with slow, powerful physical attackers that benefit from the Baton Pass (Armaldo, Hariyama, etc.). Does NOT pair well with other setup sweepers — pick one role.

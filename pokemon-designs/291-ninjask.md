# Ninjask

**Build Identity:** Ninjask is a Speed Boost sweeper that gets faster every turn — after 2-3 turns, nothing outpaces it. It can sweep directly with Swords Dance + X-Scissor, or Baton Pass a massive Speed stack (and Swords Dance) to a powerful teammate.

## Ability: Speed Boost

Speed +1 at end of every turn. Vanilla and kept as-is — it IS the identity. After 2 turns, Ninjask outruns almost everything. Fury Cutter's doubling BP synergizes perfectly: the longer you stay in, the faster AND harder you hit simultaneously.

## Ideal Items

1. **No item** — Enables Acrobatics at full 110 BP. Pure sweeper set with no held item.
2. **Salac Berry** — Additional +1 Speed at 25% HP for Baton Pass. Stack it with Speed Boost.
3. **Focus Sash** — Guarantees survival to get at least one Speed Boost off.

## Core Strategy

**Sweep mode:** Aerial Ace / X-Scissor → Swords Dance when safe → sweep with 100 Atk + SD
**Baton Pass mode:** Stay in 2-3 turns → Swords Dance → Baton Pass massive Speed stack to a heavy hitter
**Fury Cutter patience:** Turn 1 = 40 BP, turn 2 = 80, turn 3 = 160. Speed Boost stacks simultaneously.

At max Speed Boost, Ninjask is uncatchable and Baton Pass delivers that Speed to anyone on the team.

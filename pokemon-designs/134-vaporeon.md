# Vaporeon

**Build Identity:** A bulky Water-type tank that heals passively with Water Absorb and Aqua Ring, walls physical threats with Acid Armor, and delivers strong Water STAB early.

## Ability
**Water Absorb** — Immune to Water moves, restores 1/4 HP. Passive recovery stacks with Aqua Ring (1/16 per turn).

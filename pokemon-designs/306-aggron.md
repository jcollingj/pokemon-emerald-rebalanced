# Aggron

**Type:** Steel / Rock
**Ability:** Heavy Metal / Sturdy / Rock Head (hidden)

## Build Identity

Aggron is an **armored titan** that uses **Heavy Metal** (360kg → 720kg) to fire 120 BP Heavy Slam at virtually any opponent, then stacks Iron Defense to make Body Press a nuclear Fighting coverage option off its 180 Def. The fantasy: "I am the heaviest, most armored thing on the field. My weight and my armor ARE my weapons."

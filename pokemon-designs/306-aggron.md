## Build Identity

Slow physical wall with nuclear Head Smash. 180 Def laughs at physical attackers. Rock Head + Head Smash (150 BP Rock STAB, zero recoil) is the endgame payoff. Rock Polish enables a surprise +2 Speed sweeper mode.

## Items

1. **Choice Band** — Head Smash × 1.5 = 225 effective BP, zero recoil
2. Leftovers — passive recovery for wall mode
3. Assault Vest — patches SpD for mixed defensive coverage

## Core Strategy

Switch into physical threats that can't crack 180 Def. Fire off Head Smash or Double-Edge at full power. Rock Polish into a surprise sweeper if needed. Filter (hidden) reduces 4x weaknesses to 2x, enabling survivability vs Fighting/Ground.

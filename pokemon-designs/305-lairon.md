# Lairon

**Type:** Steel / Rock
**Ability:** Heavy Metal / Sturdy / Rock Head (hidden)
**Evolution:** → Aggron at L42

## Build Identity

Lairon is the transition stage. At L32 it unlocks **Heavy Slam** — the core weight-based STAB move. At 720kg (Heavy Metal), Heavy Slam hits ~120 BP against nearly every opponent. L36 unlocks **Body Press**, the Def-to-damage payoff that rewards every Iron Defense the player set up.

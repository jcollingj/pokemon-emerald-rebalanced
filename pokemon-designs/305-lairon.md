# Lairon

## Build Identity

Lairon is the mid-form of Aggron. Iron Head (Steel STAB + 30% flinch) comes online at L21, and Double-Edge at L27 hits for 120 BP with zero recoil. The build is fully real now.

## Ability

**Rock Head** — Prevents all recoil damage. Double-Edge at L27 costs nothing.

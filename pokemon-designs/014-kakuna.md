Cocoon form. Cannot attack. Exists to reach L10 and evolve into Beedrill.

| Level | Move | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| 7 | Harden | +1 Defense — survive long enough to evolve |

Evolves to Beedrill at **L10**. Carries all moves learned as Weedle (including Poison Powder).

# Seadra

Mid-stage sniper. 105 SpA with Sniper's passive +1 crit stage means Snipe Shot crits 50% of the time with no setup, making Seadra a real mid-game threat. Picks up Ice Beam and Dragon Pulse for coverage, Hydro Pump at Lv 36 as a capstone before evolving.

**Stats:** 55/65/95/105/45/85 (BST 450, +10 from vanilla)
**Ability:** Sniper (passive +1 crit stage + 2.25x crit damage) / Swift Swim / Damp (HA)
**Evolves:** Lv 36 into Kingdra (trade evo removed)

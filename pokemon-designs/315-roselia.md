# Roselia

**Build Identity:** Roselia is a priority drain mage that uses Triage to give +3 priority to all draining moves, making Giga Drain a 112.5 effective priority STAB that constantly heals it.

**Key changes:**
- Ability: Triage (drain moves get +3 priority)
- SpA: 100 -> 110, HP: 50 -> 60, SpD: 80 -> 90, Atk: 60 -> 40 (dumped)
- BST: 400 -> 415
- Learnset built around drain moves (Mega Drain L8, Giga Drain L27, Draining Kiss L44) plus Poison coverage (Venoshock L24, Sludge Bomb L36)
- Priority Leech Seed at L16 creates oppressive sustain loop

**Niche:** The only special attacker with priority drain. Outsustains opponents through constant healing while bypassing speed with Triage. Distinct from Breloom (physical Technician), Sceptile (Unburden speed), and Ludicolo (Rain Dish stall).

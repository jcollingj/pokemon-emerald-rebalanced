# Horsea

Critical hit sniper baby stage. Sniper's passive +1 crit stage means Snipe Shot (learned at Lv 8) crits 50% of the time with zero setup. Focus Energy at Lv 16 is an optional upgrade for guaranteed crits, but the build works immediately.

**Stats:** 30/40/70/75/25/60 (BST 300, +5 from vanilla)
**Ability:** Sniper (passive +1 crit stage + 2.25x crit damage) / Swift Swim / Damp (HA)
**Evolves:** Lv 16 into Seadra

See [full design doc](../docs/game-design/horsea.md) for details.

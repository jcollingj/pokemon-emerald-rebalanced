# Flareon

**Build Identity:** A physical Fire attacker that uses Guts + Flame Orb to turn status into a 1.5x Attack multiplier, then nukes with Flare Blitz and Superpower. 130 Attack is the highest in the Eeveelution family.

## Abilities
- **Ability 1: Guts** — 1.5x Attack when statused. Flame Orb: 130 Atk × 1.5 = 195 effective Attack
- **Ability 2: Flash Fire** — Immune to Fire, boosts Fire moves when hit by one
- **Hidden: Reckless** — +20% on recoil moves (Flare Blitz becomes even more devastating)

## Core Strategy
Equip Flame Orb → burn activates Guts (+50% Attack) → Flare Blitz: 120 BP × 1.5 Guts × STAB = massive damage at 130 Atk. Superpower handles Normal/Rock/Ice/Steel that resist Fire. With Reckless (hidden), Flare Blitz is even more powerful.

## The Math
- Guts + Flame Orb: 130 Atk × 1.5 = **195 effective Attack**
- Flare Blitz (120 BP × STAB 1.5 × Guts 1.5) = **270 effective BP**
- With Reckless (hidden): Flare Blitz BP × 1.2 = **144 BP base before Guts/STAB**

## Top Items
1. **Flame Orb** — Activates Guts, makes Flareon a physical monster
2. **Choice Band** — 130 Atk × 1.5 without needing to take burn damage
3. **Life Orb** — Flexible moveset with 1.3x damage

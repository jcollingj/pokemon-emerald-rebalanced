# Seaking

Sustain horn specialist with custom ability **Lancer** (1.3x to piercing/horn moves). 6 types of horn coverage: Waterfall (Water), Megahorn (Bug), Drill Run (Ground), Poison Jab (Poison), Smart Strike (Steel), Horn Leech (Grass). Horn Leech heals 50% of damage dealt -- combined with Life Orb, Seaking heals more than the recoil costs. Stats buffed to 473 BST (+23): 90/100/75/55/85/68. Bulky and self-sufficient, not fast. See full design: `docs/game-design/goldeen.md`.

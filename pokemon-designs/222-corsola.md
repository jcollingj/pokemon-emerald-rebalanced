# Corsola

Corsola is a **Hazard Support Pivot** that uses **Regenerator** to repeatedly switch in, set Stealth Rock, fish for Scald burns, and Flip Turn out — healing 33% HP every time it leaves. Water/Rock typing, 85/100/100 mixed bulk, and 450 BST make it a durable single-stage support Pokemon. It fills the team glue role: it doesn't win games itself, but it makes everything around it better through hazards, burns, and endless pivoting.

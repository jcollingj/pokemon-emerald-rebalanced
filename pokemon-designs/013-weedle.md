# Weedle

Pre-evolution of Beedrill. Appears on Bug Catcher teams on Route 102 and in Petalburg Woods.

## Key Moves

| Level | Move | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| 1 | Poison Sting | STAB intro, 30% poison chance |
| 1 | String Shot | Utility — slows opponent |
| 5 | Poison Powder | **Synergy unlocked** — poisons target for Merciless crits |

Evolves to Kakuna at **L7**. Carries Poison Powder into evolution.

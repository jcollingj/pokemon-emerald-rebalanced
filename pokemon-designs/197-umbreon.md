# Umbreon

**Build Identity:** A nearly unkillable Dark-type wall that uses Prankster to give priority to Wish, Toxic, and Confuse Ray — wearing down opponents while healing itself with near-infinite recovery.

## Abilities
- **Ability 1: Synchronize** — Passes status conditions to opponents
- **Ability 2: Synchronize**
- **Hidden: Prankster** — Status moves gain +1 priority (Wish, Toxic, Confuse Ray all go first)

## Core Strategy
Prankster gives priority to Wish, Toxic, and Confuse Ray. Toxic poisons the opponent → they chip down while Umbreon heals with priority Wish or Moonlight. Confuse Ray adds additional chip. Foul Play punishes physical sweepers trying to break through. With 105 HP / 110 Def / 130 SpD, almost nothing OHKOs it.

## The Wall Math
- 105 HP × 110 Def × 130 SpD = near-unkillable wall
- Prankster Wish: heal before opponent can attack
- Prankster Toxic: poison before they can status you
- Foul Play: uses opponent's 130+ Attack against them

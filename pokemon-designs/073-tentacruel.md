# Tentacruel

Water/Poison trapping poison tank. Poison Heal + Toxic Orb heals 1/8 HP per turn, immune to other status. Whirlpool (buffed: traps + lowers Atk) locks opponents in while Toxic/Scald grinds them out. Stats: 90/70/75/80/120/85 (520 BST). Speed dropped from 100 to 85 to reflect tank identity over pivot. 120 SpDef walls special attackers, Whirlpool Atk drop + Scald burns handle physical ones. Rain Dish as ability 2 for rain teams (1/8 HP + 1.5x Def in rain). No Toxic Spikes.

See [design doc](../../docs/game-design/tentacool.md) for full details.

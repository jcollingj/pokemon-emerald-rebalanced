# Beautifly

Tinted Lens Quiver Dance sweeper. Resisted moves deal neutral damage, so after one Quiver Dance (+SpA/+SpD/+Spe), nothing walls Beautifly. 110 SpA / 80 Spe with Bug Buzz + Hurricane + Giga Drain coverage. Sleep Powder enables setup. Glass cannon physically (60/50 HP/Def) but snowballs fast once dancing.

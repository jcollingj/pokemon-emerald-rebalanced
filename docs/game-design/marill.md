# Marill / Azumarill - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy
Azumarill is a physical water wallbreaker that uses Huge Power to double its Attack stat. 50 base Atk becomes 100 effective — and with Belly Drum available, it can reach truly absurd levels. Water/Fairy typing gives excellent offensive coverage and defensive resistances. Where Mudkip tanks and Poliwag speed-sweeps, Azumarill just hits incredibly hard.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Huge Power** — Doubles Attack stat. 50 Atk → 100 effective. Core identity.
- **Ability 2: Thick Fat** — Halves Fire and Ice damage. Solid defensive option.
- **Hidden: Sap Sipper** — Immune to Grass, +1 Atk when hit by Grass. Covers the Grass weakness.

Belly Drum Aqua Jet is the combo. Max Attack, then priority sweep. Same pattern as Zigzagoon but with Water/Fairy STAB instead of Normal.

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Band** — 110 Atk × 1.5 = 165 effective. Liquidation/Play Rough hit like trucks without setup.
2. **Sitrus Berry** — Heals 25% HP after Belly Drum (which costs 50%). Leaves Azumarill at 75% HP and +6 Atk.
3. **Mystic Water** — 1.2x Water moves. Aqua Jet becomes even scarier as priority.

## Core Strategy

### Choice Band Wallbreaker
1. Switch in (bulky enough to take hits)
2. Liquidation/Play Rough/Ice Punch — pick the right coverage
3. 165 effective Atk with STAB = massive damage without setup
4. Aqua Jet for priority finishing

### Belly Drum Sweeper
1. Switch in on something passive
2. Belly Drum (lose 50% HP, gain +6 Atk = ~660 effective Atk)
3. Aqua Jet priority sweeps — +1 priority, 660 Atk, Water STAB
4. Play Rough for Fairy STAB if you don't need priority
5. Sitrus Berry heals back 25% after Belly Drum

### vs Zigzagoon (who also has Belly Drum)
- Zigzagoon has Extreme Speed (+2 priority) but Normal STAB only
- Azumarill has Aqua Jet (+1 priority) but Water/Fairy dual STAB
- Azumarill is bulkier (110 HP vs 50 HP) and easier to set up
- Different types, different coverage — both are viable

## Team Synergy

- Benefits from Pelipper's Drizzle — rain boosts Water moves + Aqua Jet
- Covers Fire/Rock/Dark types that threaten Breloom
- Play Rough Fairy STAB covers Dragon/Fighting/Dark
- Bulky enough to switch in multiple times per game

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Fire, Rock, Dark, Dragon, Fighting (Water/Fairy coverage covers most of the game)
**Weak vs:** Grass, Electric, Poison. Steel resists Fairy.
**Key move:** Aqua Jet priority means faster Pokemon can't revenge kill after Belly Drum

# Caterpie / Metapod / Butterfree - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy

Butterfree is the Sleep-Setup Sweeper. Compound Eyes turns all powder moves to 97.5% accuracy — Sleep Powder becomes nearly guaranteed. The moment Butterfree evolves at L10 it has Sleep Powder. By L14 it has Quiver Dance. The loop is complete in four levels: sleep the target, dance for free, sweep.

**Niche vs Beedrill:** Beedrill poisons then crits (physical combo). Butterfree sleeps then sweeps (special setup). Distinct roles.

## Ability Changes

### Caterpie
- **Ability 1: Compound Eyes** — Line theme established from the start. Sets up the Butterfree identity.
- **Ability 2: Shield Dust** — Prevents secondary effects from flinch moves, sandstorm chip, etc.

### Metapod
- **Ability 1: Shell Armor** — No critical hits. The cocoon is indestructible. Thematic.
- **Ability 2: Shed Skin** — 33% chance to heal status each turn. Can Harden off burns/poison.

### Butterfree
- **Ability 1: Compound Eyes** — Sleep Powder becomes 97.5%. The core identity.
- **Ability 2: Tinted Lens** — Alt build: skip sleep entirely, Hurricane becomes unresisted. Specs + Tinted Lens + Hurricane hits neutral on every type.

## Core Strategy

### The Setup Loop
1. **Sleep Powder** — 97.5% accurate (Compound Eyes). Enemy sleeps.
2. **Quiver Dance** — free +1 SpA / SpD / Speed while they're helpless.
3. **Bug Buzz / Hurricane** — sweep. After one Quiver Dance, Butterfree hits 180 SpA at 157 Speed. Nothing is safe.

### Ideal Items

1. **Leftovers** — sustains while enemy sleeps. 1-2 free Quiver Dances make Butterfree nearly unkillable during the setup phase.
2. **Big Root** — pairs with Dream Eater (via TM): sleep → drain HP while they're helpless. Sleep is permanent recovery fuel.
3. **Choice Specs** — Tinted Lens alt build: skip sleep, Hurricane everything. Nothing resists it. 120 × 1.5 (Specs) off 120 SpA = devastation.

## Compound Eyes Math — Why This Works

After **one Quiver Dance**:
- SpA: 120 → 180
- Speed: 105 → 157 (outspeeds virtually everything)
- Hurricane: 130 BP, 91% accurate, off 180 SpA = dominant

After **two Quiver Dances** (still sleeping):
- SpA: 120 → 240
- Speed: 105 → 210 (nothing outspeeds this)
- The game is over.

## Team Synergy

- Pairs with physical sweepers who appreciate Tailwind or sleep support
- Sleep Powder removes a threat for any follow-up Pokemon to clean
- Quiver Dance SpD boost makes Butterfree surprisingly tanky on the special side mid-sweep
- U-Turn users appreciate a sleep lead → pivot out → bring in attacker safely

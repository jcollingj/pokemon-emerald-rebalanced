# Hoothoot / Noctowl - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy
Noctowl is a bulky special attacker that uses Tinted Lens to eliminate resistances. "Not very effective" moves deal normal damage — nothing walls Noctowl. Where Pidgeot is fast and hits hard, Noctowl is slow and impossible to resist. The anti-wall.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Tinted Lens** — "Not very effective" moves deal normal damage instead of half. Core identity — nothing resists Noctowl.
- **Ability 2: Insomnia** — Can't be put to sleep. Solid utility.
- **Hidden: Keen Eye** — Accuracy can't be lowered.

## Why Tinted Lens Works

Normal special attackers are walled by type resistances. Noctowl doesn't care:

| Move | vs Normal Target | vs Resistant Target (vanilla) | vs Resistant Target (Tinted Lens) |
|------|-------------------|------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Air Slash (75) | 112.5 STAB | 56.25 (halved) | **112.5 (full!)** |
| Psychic (90) | 90 | 45 | **90** |
| Shadow Ball (80) | 80 | 40 | **80** |

With Tinted Lens, you don't need "coverage" — every move hits everything at full power. You just pick your strongest move and fire.

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Specs** — 105 SpA × 1.5 = 157.5 effective SpA. Nothing resists + Choice Specs = everything takes massive damage regardless of type.
2. **Life Orb** — 1.3x with flexible moveset. Tinted Lens means you always hit hard, Life Orb makes it harder.
3. **Leftovers** — Sustain for Calm Mind sets. 110 HP + Roost + Leftovers = outlasts opponents.

## Core Strategy

### Choice Specs Wallbreaker
1. Switch in (110 HP + 105 SpD handles special hits)
2. Fire Hurricane/Psychic/Shadow Ball — doesn't matter what they resist, Tinted Lens means full damage
3. No prediction needed — just pick the strongest available move
4. The anti-wall. Nothing switches in safely.

### Calm Mind Tank
1. Switch in on something passive
2. Calm Mind (+1 SpA, +1 SpD) — now 105 SpA → 157.5, 105 SpD → 157.5
3. Roost for sustain
4. After 2-3 Calm Minds, Noctowl is unkillable on the special side and hits everything for full damage

## Team Synergy

- Handles Steel/Rock types that wall Pidgeot's Flying STAB
- Bulky enough to pivot into special attacks for the team
- Hypnosis support puts threats to sleep
- Calm Mind + Tinted Lens breaks through stall that Breloom/Gallade can't

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Literally everything — Tinted Lens means nothing resists. Especially effective vs stall/defensive teams.
**Weak vs:** Physical attackers exploit lower Def (65). Rock, Electric, Ice, Dark hit super effectively.
**vs Pidgeot:** Pidgeot is faster and hits harder turn 1. Noctowl is bulkier, can't be walled, and wins long games.

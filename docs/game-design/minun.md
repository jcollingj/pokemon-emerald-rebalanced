# Minun #312 — The Bodyguard Tank

## Build Identity

Minun is a dedicated doubles support Pokemon that exists to absorb hits for Plusle. High bulk, Follow Me, and Friend Guard make Plusle nearly unkillable. Minun doesn't deal damage — it enables Plusle to deal devastating damage safely.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Friend Guard** — Reduces damage dealt to allies by 25% in doubles. Stacks with Minun's bulk: a 95 HP / 95 SpD tank with 25% damage reduction means Plusle takes barely anything through the guard.
- **Ability 2: Minus** — 1.5× SpA when a partner with Plus is on the field. Backup offensive identity — Minun can become a modest attacker if Plusle needs support on the other slot.
- **Hidden: Unaware** — Ignores the opponent's stat changes when calculating damage taken and dealt. Minun laughs at Swords Dance / Calm Mind setups while tanking hits. Impossible to overwhelm through stat boosts.

HP and both defenses dramatically boosted. SpA and Speed gutted. Minun is not here to attack. 95/75/95 bulk is a real defensive profile — combined with Friend Guard (-25% to ally damage), Plusle becomes incredibly hard to remove.

Speed lowered to 55 so Minun moves before Plusle (Speed 50) but after most threats — Minun needs to redirect attacks before opponents act when going last isn't ideal. Consider Trick Room synergy or just accepting that Follow Me sometimes gets set up on turn 2.

## Ideal Items

1. **Leftovers** — Passive 1/16 HP recovery each turn. Keeps Minun alive long enough for multiple Wish cycles. The tank wants longevity.
2. **Light Clay** — Extends Light Screen (and Reflect if added) to 8 turns instead of 5. Massive value for a support Pokemon.
3. **Sitrus Berry** — Instant heal when below 50% HP. Emergency recovery when Leftovers isn't enough after a big hit gets through.

## Core Strategy

### The Bodyguard Protocol
1. Turn 1: Minun → Follow Me. All single-target attacks hit Minun (95/75/95 bulk tanks them). Plusle → Nasty Plot safely.
2. Turn 2: Minun → Fake Tears on scariest special attacker. Plusle → Discharge (spread, hits both opponents, now into −2 SpD).
3. Turn 3: Minun → Wish (targeting Minun for later self-heal or Plusle if low). Plusle → Thunderbolt (massive damage into −2 SpD target).
4. Repeat — Wish heals back to full, Follow Me again, second nuke cycle.

### What "Protect Plusle" Means in Practice
- Follow Me eats Choice Band Earthquakes, Surfs, Fire Blasts — Minun's 95 HP / 75 Def handles physical hits, 95/95 SpD handles special hits.
- Friend Guard means even when Plusle does take a hit (spread move, chain), it takes 25% less.
- Charm + Minun's physical bulk means even physical attackers trying to break through take −2 Atk.

### If Minun Falls
If Minun is KO'd, Plusle is now exposed — 50 Speed means it's outsped by everything. The player's win condition if they can eliminate Minun first. But Minun's 95 HP + 75/95 defenses make it genuinely hard to knock out quickly without a super-effective move.

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Physical attackers (Charm + 75 Def), setup sweepers (Encore, Unaware HA), anything that doesn't carry a spread move.
**Weak vs:** Poison types (immune to Charm drop), Steel types (immune to Fake Tears), super-effective physical hits (Fighting/Ground bypasses HP + Def).
**Key vulnerability:** Spread moves bypass Follow Me. Earthquake, Surf, Rock Slide — Plusle takes full damage despite Follow Me redirection.

## Team Synergy

- **Needs Plusle** — Minun's Friend Guard and Follow Me exist to protect Plusle. The pair is the whole point.
- Pairs with Fake Tears setters on the bench to stack SpD drops if the opponent switches.
- Light Screen + high SpD = special attackers often can't remove Minun in one or two hits.
- Wish passes to Plusle if Minun is about to faint — buy Plusle one more nuke cycle.

## Amy & Liv Doubles Battle Design

Minun is the scarier Pokemon in this fight, counterintuitively. The player sees Plusle's huge SpA and focuses on it — but Plusle is hiding behind Minun the whole time.

- **Threat identification:** Players without Earthquake or spread moves will struggle to hit Plusle.
- **The Follow Me bait:** Turn 1 Follow Me + Nasty Plot forces the player to use their spread move immediately or lose control.
- **The Wish cycle:** If the player takes 2+ turns to kill Minun, Wish has already healed Minun. The player needs to either KO both in the same turn window or accept the sustained threat.

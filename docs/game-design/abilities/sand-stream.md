# Sand Stream

## Effect
Summons sandstorm for 5 turns on switch-in. Extended to 8 turns with Smooth Rock. Sandstorm deals 1/16 max HP per turn to non-Rock/Ground/Steel types and grants Rock types a 1.5x SpDef boost.

## Key Interactions
- Tyranitar's 100 SpDef becomes effectively 150 in sand
- Combined with Stealth Rock, each opponent switch costs 18.75%+ HP
- Enables Sand Force teammates (Probopass, Geodude)

## Pokemon Using This
- **Larvitar / Pupitar / Tyranitar** -- Sand-setting bulky attacker. The entire line gets Sand Stream from L1. Tyranitar at 600 BST with 134 Atk and effective 150 SpDef in sand is one of the bulkiest Pokemon in the game. Larvitar appears on Roxanne's gym team as a Sand Stream lead.

# Aerilate

## Effect
Normal-type moves become Flying-type and receive a 1.2x power boost. Allows Normal moves like Return, Quick Attack, Double-Edge, and Extreme Speed to become powerful Flying STAB.

## Key Move Interactions
- Return (102 BP) becomes 122 BP Flying STAB = 183 effective
- Double-Edge (120 BP) becomes 144 BP Flying STAB = 216 effective
- Extreme Speed (80 BP) becomes 96 BP Flying with +2 priority = 144 effective
- Quick Attack (40 BP) becomes 48 BP Flying with +1 priority

## Pokemon Using This
- **Pidgeot** -- Fast physical sweeper (110 Speed, 105 Atk). Every Normal move becomes devastating Flying STAB. The "every move benefits" pattern. Silk Scarf stacks with Aerilate for even more damage.

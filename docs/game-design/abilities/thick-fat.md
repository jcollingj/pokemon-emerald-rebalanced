# Thick Fat

## Effect
Reduces damage from Fire and Ice-type moves by 50%. Provides two effective resistances without changing typing.

## Pokemon Using This
- **Azumarill** -- Secondary ability (Ability 2). Defensive option alongside Huge Power. Halves Fire and Ice damage, covering two of Azumarill's potential weaknesses.

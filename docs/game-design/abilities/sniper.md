# Sniper

## Effect (Modified)
- Critical hits deal 2.25x damage (1.5x on top of the normal 1.5x crit multiplier) -- unchanged from vanilla
- **NEW:** Grants +1 critical hit stage passively (like a built-in Super Luck)

## Why Modified
The +1 crit stage makes Focus Energy + Scope Lens setups even more consistent. With the passive +1, Focus Energy alone reaches +3 crit stages (guaranteed crits on every move), removing the need for Scope Lens and freeing the item slot for Choice Specs or Life Orb.

## Crit Stage Math (with modified Sniper)
- Sniper passive: +1 crit stage
- Focus Energy: +2 crit stages
- Total: +3 = guaranteed crits on every move without needing Scope Lens

## Pokemon Using This
- **Kingdra** -- Critical hit sniper. Water/Dragon with 115 SpA and 90 Speed. Focus Energy + Sniper = guaranteed 2.25x crits. Snipe Shot at 270 effective BP, Draco Meteor at 439 effective BP (crits ignore SpA drops from Draco Meteor). The "every hit is a headshot" build.
- **Fearow** -- Hidden ability alternative to Reckless. Pairs with high-crit moves like Drill Peck and Slash.

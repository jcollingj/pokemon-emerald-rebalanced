# Toxic Boost

## Effect
When the Pokemon is poisoned, its Attack stat is boosted by 1.5x. Hold Toxic Orb to self-poison for a permanent 50% Attack boost at the cost of 1/8 HP per turn from poison damage.

## Key Interactions
- 120 base Atk x 1.5 = 180 effective Attack on Nidoking
- Costs 1/8 HP per turn (about 12 HP with 90 base HP) -- worth it for 50% Atk boost
- Toxic Orb activates after 1 turn
- Prevents other status (already poisoned)

## Pokemon Using This
- **Nidoking** -- Physical wallbreaker (120 Atk -> 180 effective, 95 Speed). Gunk Shot at 180 STAB power off 180 Atk is devastating. Earthquake, Megahorn, Superpower, elemental punches for coverage. The offensive counterpart to Nidoqueen's defensive Poison Heal.

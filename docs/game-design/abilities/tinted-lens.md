# Tinted Lens

## Effect
"Not very effective" moves deal normal damage instead of half. Nothing resists Noctowl -- you just pick your strongest move and fire.

## Why It Works
Normal special attackers get walled by type resistances. With Tinted Lens, Air Slash against a Rock type still deals full damage. No need for "coverage" moves -- every move hits at full power against everything.

## Pokemon Using This
- **Noctowl** -- Bulky special attacker (110 HP, 105 SpA, 105 SpD). The anti-wall. Calm Mind sets become truly unstoppable since nothing can resist the boosted attacks. Choice Specs variant wallbreaks without needing to predict.

# Poison Heal

## Effect
When the Pokemon is poisoned, it heals 1/8 max HP per turn instead of taking poison damage. Hold Toxic Orb to self-poison for permanent passive healing.

## Key Interactions
- Toxic Orb activates after 1 turn, then permanent 1/8 healing
- Prevents other status conditions (already poisoned = can't be burned/paralyzed/etc.)
- Stacks with Protect for free healing turns
- Stacks with Recover/Rest for layered sustain

## Pokemon Using This
- **Nidoqueen** -- Special defense tank (105 HP, 95 Def, 120 SpD). Heals ~13 HP/turn with Toxic Orb. Combined with Recover and Protect, becomes nearly unkillable. Fires back with Earth Power and Sludge Wave off 85 SpA.
- **Toxapex** -- Defensive stall wall. Self-sustaining tank with Baneful Bunker + Toxic Spikes. Vanilla Merciless replaced with Poison Heal for sustain identity.

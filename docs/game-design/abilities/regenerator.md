# Regenerator

## Effect
Heals 33% of max HP when the Pokemon switches out. Enables infinite pivoting -- every switch-out is free healing.

## Key Interactions
- Flip Turn = attack + switch + 33% heal in one move
- Stacks with Leftovers/Black Sludge for HP-positive switching
- Over 3-4 switches, effectively doubles the Pokemon's total HP pool
- Rewards pivot-heavy playstyles with constant repositioning

## Pokemon Using This
- **Corsola** -- Hazard support pivot. Water/Rock typing with 85/100/100 bulk. Sets Stealth Rock, spreads Scald burns, Flip Turns out for 33% heal. Comes back later fully healthy to do it again. The "I never go away" glue Pokemon.
- **Tentacruel** -- Fast poison pivot tank. Water/Poison with 120 SpDef and 100 Speed. Toxic Spikes on entry, Flip Turn out to heal, cycle back in on special attacks. Black Sludge + Regenerator makes every switch-in/switch-out cycle HP-positive. The tempo-focused tank.

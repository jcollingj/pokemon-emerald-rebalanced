# Trace

## Effect (Modified)
On switch-in, copies the opponent's ability **AND** gets +1 Special Attack. The guaranteed +1 SpA makes every switch-in valuable regardless of what ability is copied.

## Why Modified
Vanilla Trace is too random -- sometimes amazing, sometimes useless. The +1 SpA on entry creates a reliable pivoting playstyle. Combined with Teleport, Gardevoir can loop: Trace in (+1 SpA) -> attack -> Teleport out -> Trace in (+1 SpA again). Each cycle stacks power.

## Key Interactions
- 130 base SpA + Trace's +1 on entry = 195 effective SpA
- After two pivots = 260 effective SpA
- Teleport (-6 priority) enables safe pivoting
- Eject Button forces pivot after hit, triggering Trace on re-entry

## Pokemon Using This
- **Gardevoir** -- Empathic special pivot. Psychic/Fairy typing with 130 SpA. Pivots in and out with Teleport to stack SpA boosts. Moonblast, Shadow Ball, Thunderbolt for coverage. The "I understand you" fantasy.

# Pickup

## Effect (Modified)
- **Out of battle:** Picks up items after battle (unchanged from vanilla)
- **In battle (NEW):** Takes half damage from attacks when at full HP and not holding an item

## Why Modified
Vanilla Pickup had zero in-battle effect. The rework creates a Multiscale-at-full-HP pattern that enables Zigzagoon's Belly Drum strategy -- survive the first hit at half damage, then Belly Drum safely.

## Key Interactions
- Requires NO held item for the in-battle effect to work
- Only activates at full HP (one-time damage reduction)
- Synergizes with Belly Drum: survive a hit -> Belly Drum -> sweep with Extreme Speed

## Pokemon Using This
- **Zigzagoon / Linoone** -- Belly Drum sweeper. Pickup lets Zigzagoon survive the first hit, Belly Drum (+6 Atk, +1 Speed), then sweep with Quick Attack/Extreme Speed priority. The "no item" requirement creates a unique high-risk playstyle.

# Truant

## Effect (Modified)
- Still skips every other turn (unchanged)
- **NEW:** On switch-in, the Pokemon gains +1 Attack and +1 Defense

## Why Modified
Vanilla Truant is pure downside -- Slaking only attacks half the time with no compensation beyond raw stats. The rework adds +1 Atk/+1 Def on entry, making Slaking a "siege cannon" that enters already boosted. The Defense boost lets it survive the loaf turn. The Attack boost makes every hit devastating from turn 1.

## Key Interactions
- 160 Atk x 1.5 (+1 stage) = 240 effective Attack on entry
- 100 Def x 1.5 (+1 stage) = 150 effective Defense to survive loaf turns
- Giga Impact/Hyper Beam recharge aligns with Truant skip = zero wasted turns
- Choice Band: 160 x 1.5 (Truant) x 1.5 (Band) = 360 effective Attack
- Yawn on active turn -> opponent sleeps on loaf turn -> free Giga Impact

## Pokemon Using This
- **Slaking** -- Siege cannon. 150 HP / 160 Atk / 100 Def at 660 BST. Enters with +1 Atk/Def, fires Giga Impact (225 effective STAB) every other turn. The hardest single hit in the game. Only attacks every 2 turns but each hit is potentially a OHKO.

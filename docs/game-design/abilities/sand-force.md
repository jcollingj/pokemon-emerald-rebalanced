# Sand Force

## Effect
Rock, Ground, and Steel-type moves deal 1.3x damage while sandstorm is active. Does not affect the user's immunity to sandstorm chip damage (only Rock/Ground/Steel types are naturally immune).

## Boosted Moves (Probopass example)
Power Gem (80 -> 104), Flash Cannon (80 -> 104), Earth Power (90 -> 117), Stone Edge (100 -> 130)

## Pokemon Using This
- **Probopass** -- Sand fortress. Rock/Steel typing gives 9 resistances. 145 Def makes Body Press devastating, 150 SpDef in sand becomes effectively 225. Sand Force turns its modest 75 SpA into real threat level. Pairs with Tyranitar's Sand Stream.
- **Geodude** -- Hidden ability. 1.3x Rock/Ground/Steel moves in sand pairs with Larvitar's Sand Stream for a sand-based team core.

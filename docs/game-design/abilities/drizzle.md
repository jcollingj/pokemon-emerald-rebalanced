# Drizzle

## Effect
Summons rain for 5 turns on switch-in. Extended to 8 turns with Damp Rock.

## Rain Effects
- Water moves get 1.5x boost
- Fire moves get 0.5x reduction
- Hurricane never misses
- Thunder never misses
- Activates Swift Swim, Rain Dish for teammates

## Pokemon Using This
- **Pelipper** -- The rain team's engine. Bulky support pivot (85 HP, 105 Def, 100 SpA). Sets rain on switch-in, U-Turns out to bring in Swift Swim sweepers (Poliwag, Surskit, Omastar). Scald in rain reaches 180 effective BP with 30% burn.

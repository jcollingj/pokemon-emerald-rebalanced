# Storm Drain

## Effect
Draws in all Water-type moves (including from allies in doubles), grants full immunity, and raises the user's Special Attack by +1 stage. Each Water attack redirected = free SpA boost.

## Key Interactions
- Patches Cradily's Rock/Grass 4x Water weakness, turning it into a strength
- Each absorbed Water move = +1 SpA (91 base -> 136 at +1, 182 at +2)
- Shapes team building: switch Cradily into Water attacks for free boosts

## Pokemon Using This
- **Cradily** -- Immovable special tank. Rock/Grass typing with Storm Drain turns the biggest weakness into fuel. Plants itself with Ingrain, drains HP with Giga Drain, and fires increasingly powerful special attacks. 92 HP / 97 Def / 112 SpDef = legitimate mixed wall that gets stronger every time it absorbs Water.

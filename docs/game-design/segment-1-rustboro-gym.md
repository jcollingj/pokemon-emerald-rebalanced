3. **Petalburg City** — Meet Dad (Norman), Wally catches Ralts

5. **Rustboro Gym** — Roxanne battle (6 Pokemon sandstorm team)

## Design Notes
- Exp Share available early on Route 103
- Pikachu with Light Ball on Route 102 is a strong early pickup
- Squirtle available on Routes 103 and 104 (Water-type coverage for Roxanne)
- Nidoran line available evolved (Nidorino/Nidorina) on Route 102
- Roxanne's team has type diversity beyond Rock (Water Gun on Omanyte/Anorith, Thunderbolt on Nosepass)
- Sand Stream + Sand Force Nosepass is the ace combo

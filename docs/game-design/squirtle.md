# Squirtle - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy
Squirtle is being transformed into a pulse-move specialist with Mega Launcher ability, creating a unique coverage-based special attacker with unmatched type diversity and powerful boosted pulse moves.

## Stat Changes - Full Evolution Line

### Squirtle (Base Form)

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced | Change |
|------|---------|------------|--------|
| HP | 44 | **60** | +16 |
| Attack | 48 | 48 | - |
| Defense | 65 | 65 | - |
| Sp.Attack | 50 | **65** | +15 |
| Sp.Defense | 64 | **70** | +6 |
| Speed | 43 | 43 | - |
| **BST** | **314** | **351** | **+37** |

**Rationale:**
- HP boost for survivability as early-game tank
- Sp.Attack boost for viable pulse move damage
- Sp.Defense boost for special bulk
- Keep Attack/Defense/Speed unchanged (not its focus)

### Wartortle (Mid Evolution)

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced | Change |
|------|---------|------------|--------|

| Sp.Defense | 80 | **90** | +10 |

**Rationale:**
- Continued HP scaling (+16 maintained)
- Bigger Sp.Attack jump to prepare for Blastoise role
- Sp.Defense scaling maintains special tank identity
- Slight Defense bump for overall bulk

### Blastoise (Final Evolution)

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced | Change |
|------|---------|------------|--------|
| HP | 79 | **92** | +13 |
| Attack | 83 | 83 | - |
| Defense | 100 | **105** | +5 |
| Sp.Attack | 85 | **110** | +25 |
| Sp.Defense | 105 | **112** | +7 |
| Speed | 78 | 78 | - |
| **BST** | **530** | **580** | **+50** |

**Rationale:**
- **110 Sp.Attack** makes pulse moves devastating with Mega Launcher
- Maintained special bulk with 112 Sp.Defense
- Solid physical bulk with 105 Defense
- 92 HP gives good overall bulk
- Speed unchanged (not a sweeper archetype)

**Power Calculation with Mega Launcher:**
- Effective Sp.Attack with boosted moves: ~165 (110 × 1.5)
- Origin Pulse: 165 power with STAB = **247.5** effective power
- Hydro Cannon: 225 power with STAB = **337.5** effective power

## Core Strategy

**The Pulse Specialist:**
1. **Early Game:** Water Pulse (90 effective power with Mega Launcher) + Aura Sphere (never-miss Fighting coverage)
2. **Utility:** Rapid Spin for hazard removal, Aqua Ring for sustain
3. **Mid Game:** Add Dragon Pulse and Dark Pulse for perfect coverage
4. **Support:** Rain Dance for rain team synergy
5. **Late Game (Blastoise):** Origin Pulse (165 effective power!) and Hydro Cannon (225 effective power!)

**Why This Works:**
- Mega Launcher makes all pulse moves 50% stronger
- Perfect type coverage: Water/Fighting/Dragon/Dark/Terrain-based
- Aura Sphere never misses - reliable damage always
- Rapid Spin provides utility (hazard removal + speed boost)
- Aqua Ring + bulk = sustainable damage dealer
- Origin Pulse and Hydro Cannon become nuclear options

## Evolution Progression

### Squirtle → Wartortle (Level 16)
- **Early Game Foundation:** Sets up pulse-move specialist role
- Water Pulse + Aura Sphere by level 14 = immediate viability
- Bulky enough to survive early battles (60 HP, 70 Sp.Def)
- Rapid Spin for utility, Aqua Ring for sustain

### Wartortle → Blastoise (Level 36)
- **Mid-Game Powerhouse:** Solid stats (85 Sp.Atk, 75 HP, 90 Sp.Def)
- Gains Dragon Pulse and Dark Pulse for perfect coverage
- Rain Dance support capability
- Transition form that's competitive in mid-game

### Blastoise (Final Form)
- **Late-Game Nuclear Option:** 110 Sp.Atk + Mega Launcher = devastating
- Learns Origin Pulse (247.5 effective power!) and Hydro Cannon (337.5 power!)
- Balanced bulk (92 HP, 105 Def, 112 Sp.Def) allows it to tank hits
- Perfect coverage with 4+ pulse moves (Water/Fighting/Dragon/Dark)
- Top-tier special attacker while maintaining utility role

**Team Support:**

- Rain Dance supports Swift Swim users (Poliwag, Surskit, Horsea)
- Aqua Ring + Rapid Spin speed boost = sustained pressure

**vs Other Water Types:**
- Poliwag/Surskit: Speed-based rain sweepers

- Tentacool: Rain Dish pivot with poison synergy

**First Gym Strategy:**

- Aura Sphere (Fighting) hits Rock types super effectively
- Rapid Spin + Aqua Ring for sustain
- Can support rain team with Rain Dance

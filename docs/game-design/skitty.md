# Skitty / Delcatty — Normalize Special Sweeper

## Build Identity

Delcatty is a Normalize special sweeper that converts every move it knows to Normal type, stacking with Normal STAB for 1.8× effective damage on nearly anything it hits.

## Ability — Normalize

Normalize converts all moves to Normal type. In Gen 7+ mode (used here), it also applies a 1.2× boost to those converted moves. Combined with Normal STAB, this means:

**All moves → 1.2× (Normalize boost) × 1.5× (STAB) = 1.8× effective damage**

Shadow Ball, Play Rough — anything you teach Delcatty becomes a Normal STAB hit. The player feels clever stacking damage multipliers. The identity: "every move in my kit hits like a STAB move."

Normalize is slot 1 (primary), so Delcatty will have it by default. Cute Charm in slot 2 gives access to a softer defensive option.

## Core Strategy

**Sweeper path:** Bring in Delcatty, use Calm Mind once or twice, then spam Hyper Voice or Return. With Normalize, even coverage moves like Play Rough hit for full 1.8× damage on everything that isn't Ghost type.

**Utility path:** Use Fake Out for guaranteed priority flinch, Sing for sleep, Wish to keep itself or a teammate healthy.

**Item path:** Slap Choice Specs and immediately threaten almost anything with 1.8× boosted Hyper Voice.

The weakness is Ghost types — Normalize can't affect Ghosts (Normal moves don't hit them, and Normalize doesn't override the immunity). Play Rough bypasses this as a Fairy move before Normalize converts it. Wait — Normalize converts Play Rough to Normal too, which means Ghost immunity applies. This is a meaningful counterplay and design tension.

## Team Synergy

- **Ghost checks**: A Dark or Ghost type partner handles the Ghost immunity wall
- **Hazard setters**: Delcatty is a cleaner, not a setter — pair with something that sets Stealth Rock first
- **Trick Room teams**: Delcatty's 95 Speed is good but not elite; Trick Room support makes it slower than intended — better on normal-speed teams

## Matchups

**Strong against:** Most types — Normalize makes everything a Normal STAB hit, which is neutral to ~10 types
**Weak against:** Ghost types (Normal moves don't hit them, so all Normalize attacks miss), Rock and Steel (Normal is not very effective)
**Notable counters:** Gengar, Dusclops, Sableye — any Ghost type hard stops Delcatty's offense

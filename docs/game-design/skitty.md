# Skitty / Delcatty — Rebalance Notes

## Build Identity

Delcatty is a **Normalize Special Sweeper** that uses Normalize (all moves → Normal type + 1.2× boost) combined with Normal STAB for **1.8× effective damage on every move in its kit**. Hyper Voice, Return, and Play Rough all hit at the same multiplier — there's no wrong move to click.

## Ability

**Normalize** (primary ability)

All moves become Normal type. In Gen 7+ mode (our config), this also grants a 1.2× power boost. Combined with Normal STAB, every move hits at 1.8× effective power. Shadow Ball becomes a 144-effective-power Normal STAB. Hyper Voice becomes a spread STAB nuke. Play Rough loses its Fairy typing but gains the 1.8× multiplier — consistent and powerful.

The player discovers this ability by:
1. L8 — Fake Out (priority flinch, demonstrates Normalize STAB)
2. L12 — Sing (status move, shows off type-neutral kit)
3. Post-Moon Stone — Hyper Voice, Calm Mind, Return unlock the full identity

## Core Strategy

Delcatty's core is simple: every move in its kit hits at 1.8× thanks to Normalize + STAB. Lead with Fake Out for free chip damage, then either set up with Calm Mind or fire off Hyper Voice for immediate power. Return is the max-friendship nuke for late-game. Wish enables it to heal on switches.

The Calm Mind set is the win condition: +1/+1 makes Delcatty's already-strong SpA into a wallbreaker, and coverage doesn't matter because every move is the same type anyway.

## Team Synergy

- Partners that can set up Calm Mind fodder are appreciated
- Pairs with screens support (Light Screen especially) to bolster SpD before setting up
- Ghost-types threaten Delcatty but Normalize makes its attacks Normal-type, so it can't hit Ghosts — this is a notable weakness to build around

## Matchups

**Strong against:** Anything that doesn't resist or is immune to Normal (no Ghost-types)
**Weak against:** Ghost types (immune to Normal moves via Normalize); Rock/Steel types reduce damage; faster threats before Calm Mind

## Evolution

Vanilla Moon Stone evolution retained. Skitty evolves into Delcatty via Moon Stone.

# Plusle - The Voltage Spike

## Design Philosophy

Plusle is a doubles attacker whose entire identity is built around the Amy & Liv fight on Route 103. Alone it's a capable Electric special attacker. **Beside Minun, it becomes a monster.** Plus activates the moment Minun is on the field, handing Plusle a free +50% SpA with no effort. Lightning Rod as the second ability lets Plusle eat any incoming Electric moves meant for Minun — and turns each one into another SpA boost. The player fighting this duo is in trouble: weaken Plusle and Minun softens your team; weaken Minun and Plusle absorbs your Electric attacks and hits back harder.

The fantasy is a pair of cheerleader Pokemon who fire each other up — and the mechanics deliver exactly that.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Plus** — Raises SpA by 50% when an ally on the field has the Minus ability. This is the core combo. Plusle + Minun = both at 1.5× SpA immediately, no setup required.
- **Ability 2: Lightning Rod** — Draws all single-target Electric moves to Plusle in doubles. Immune to Electric, gains SpA +1 when hit. Protects Minun from Electric attacks AND stacks more SpA on top of Plus.
- **Hidden: Technician** — Boosts moves 60 BP or under by 1.5×. Powers up Nuzzle (20 BP → 30), Charge Beam (50 BP → 75), Swift (60 BP → 90). Makes the early moveset punchy.

## The Math

**Plusle @ Choice Specs, Plus active, Minun on field:**

| Move | Base BP | Plus (1.5×) | Specs (1.5×) | Effective SpA | Net Power |
|------|---------|-------------|--------------|---------------|-----------|
| Discharge | 80 | 105 × 1.5 = 157 | × 1.5 = 235 | 235 | **~25,400** |
| Thunder | 110 | 105 × 1.5 = 157 | × 1.5 = 235 | 235 | **~34,800** |
| Charge Beam | 50 | 105 × 1.5 = 157 | × 1.5 = 235 | 235 | **~15,900** |

*Damage index = BP × SpA for comparison purposes.*

**Technician Nuzzle:** 20 BP × 1.5 = 30 effective. Paralyzes on contact (100%). A free speed wipe.

**Lightning Rod stacking:** Each absorbed Electric move grants SpA +1. If Plusle absorbs 2 Electric hits (Discharge from both opponents targeting Minun), Plusle hits at 105 × 1.5 (Plus) × 2.0 (+2 boost) = **315 effective SpA** before items.

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Specs** — 105 SpA → 157 (Plus) → 235 effective. Discharge off 235 SpA hits for essentially uncountable damage for early-game. Locked into one move but Plusle only needs Discharge vs most teams.
2. **Magnet** — 1.2× on Electric moves. Flexible moveset, stacks with Plus. Better for the Encore/Helping Hand support build where you need to switch moves.
3. **Life Orb** — 1.3× on everything, flexible coverage. The recoil is manageable at 65 HP if Minun keeps using Wish.

## Core Strategy

### The Cheerleader Blitz (Doubles)
1. Amy sends out Plusle + Minun together. Plus and Minus activate immediately — both at 1.5× SpA.
2. Minun opens with **Fake Tears** on the bulkier target (−2 SpD). Target is now taking 2× special damage.
3. Plusle fires **Discharge** — 80 BP spread move with 235 effective SpA into a −2 SpD target. This OHKOs almost anything that isn't a Steel or Ground type.
4. Minun uses **Helping Hand** on Plusle next turn for an additional 1.5× boost on the KO target.
5. If the opponent targets Minun with Electric: Plusle's Lightning Rod redirects it, Plusle gets +1 SpA, game over.

### The Minun Problem
When fighting Amy & Liv, the "obvious" play is kill Minun first to deactivate Plus. But:
- Minun has 95 SpD and can use Charm to cut Atk-based OHKO attempts
- Killing Minun means Plusle keeps Lightning Rod SpA stacks from earlier
- Spreading damage across both is correct — but they're both surprisingly bulky for their level

## Team Synergy

- **Mandatory partner: Minun.** No Minun = no Plus = just a 105 SpA Electric with decent speed. Good, not terrifying.
- **Ground coverage partner:** Plusle and Minun have zero answer to Ground types (Discharge is Electric, Ground immune). Amy & Liv need a non-Electric partner move or a different slot to handle Ground.
- **Wish chain:** Minun's Wish → Plusle switches out → returns healed. Keeps Plusle fresh for another nuke cycle.

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Water, Flying — Discharge hits them for neutral or super effective. Spread move means double coverage.
**Weak vs:** Ground (immune to Electric entirely), Grass (takes Discharge fine), Dragon (neutral)
**Key threat:** Ground types completely wall both Plusle and Minun. The duo needs to pray the opponent doesn't lead Ground.

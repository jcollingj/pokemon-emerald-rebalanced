# Plusle #311 — The Slow Artillery Cannon

## Build Identity

Plusle is a slow-moving siege weapon that hides behind Minun's Follow Me and fires devastating Electric attacks with an absurdly high SpA. It doesn't need speed because Minun tanks everything for it.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Plus** — 1.5× SpA when a partner with Minus is on the field. Combined with 115 base SpA, this reaches ~172 effective SpA before items — devastating.
- **Ability 2: Solar Power** — 1.5× SpA in sunshine, -1/8 HP per turn. Glass cannon mode for solo play when Minun isn't available.
- **Hidden: Technician** — Powers up moves ≤60 BP. Nuzzle (30→45), Charge Beam (50→75), ThunderShock (40→60). Enables a "chip and stack" playstyle.

## The Math

Base SpA 115 with Plus active (×1.5) = **172 effective SpA**.

| Scenario | Effective SpA | Thunderbolt EP |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Plus alone | 172 | ~155 damage est. |
| Plus + Nasty Plot (+2) | 344 | wall-breaking |
| Plus + Choice Specs | 258 | one-shot range on most threats |
| Plus + Specs + Nasty Plot | 387 | absurd |

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Specs** — 115 × 1.5 = 172, then Plus raises to ~258 effective SpA. Point at target, fire. Locked into one move but Plusle only needs one move.
2. **Life Orb** — flexibility to Nasty Plot first, then nuke everything. 1.3× boost while keeping move choice. The setup build.
3. **Focus Sash** — survives one hit if Minun falls or Follow Me gets bypassed. Insurance for when the bodyguard dies first.

## Core Strategy

### The Artillery Protocol
1. Minun leads with Follow Me — all single-target moves redirect to Minun.
2. Plusle uses Nasty Plot freely behind Follow Me protection.
3. After +2 SpA, Plusle fires Thunderbolt or Discharge into softened targets.
4. Minun uses Fake Tears to drop SpD −2 on the primary threat before Plusle fires.
5. Minun uses Wish to heal Plusle back to full between nuke cycles.

### Solo Play (Without Minun)
- Solar Power in sun: set up Sunny Day or bring a Sun setter, then Plusle hits even harder despite HP drain.
- Technician set: Charge Beam + Nuzzle for chip + SpA stacking.

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Anything not Ground-type. 258+ effective SpA Thunderbolt hits almost everything.
**Weak vs:** Ground types (immune to Electric), spread moves that bypass Follow Me, priority moves that kill Plusle while it's slow.
**Key vulnerability:** Speed 50 means Plusle is outsped by almost everything. Without Minun's Follow Me, it will be KO'd before it moves.

## Team Synergy

- **Needs Minun** — the entire slow cannon concept falls apart without Follow Me protection.
- Pairs with Tailwind setters to patch Speed in non-doubles contexts.
- Baton Pass synergy: pass Nasty Plot stacks to a physical attacker if Plusle is threatened.
- Ground-immune partners (Flying, Levitate) cover the Earthquake weakness.

## Amy & Liv Doubles Battle Design

In the Route 103 doubles trainer fight:

- **Plusle moves last** (Speed 50) but Minun always covers with Follow Me turn 1.
- Turn 1: Minun → Follow Me. Plusle → Nasty Plot (protected).
- Turn 2: Minun → Fake Tears on biggest threat. Plusle → Discharge (spread hit, +2 SpA, off 115 base with Plus).
- Turn 3: Minun → Wish. Plusle → Thunderbolt on priority target (now with −2 SpD, hits for huge damage).

**The player's dilemma:**
- Kill Minun first? Plusle still has 115 SpA but is now slow and unprotected.
- Kill Plusle first? Hard because Follow Me makes all single-target moves hit Minun.
- Use spread moves? Earthquake hits both — but the player likely doesn't have it yet at Route 103.
- Status through Follow Me? Discharge has 30% paralysis and hits both — paralysis can hit Plusle despite Follow Me.

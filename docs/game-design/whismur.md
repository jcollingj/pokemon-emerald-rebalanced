# Whismur / Loudred / Exploud - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy

Exploud is the loudest thing in the game. Punk Rock turns every sound move into a multiplied nuke — Boomburst (140 BP) × 1.3 Punk Rock × 1.5 STAB = **273 effective BP** off 105 SpA. The build is simple: get to Exploud, point it at things, and watch them evaporate. Whismur earns its keep before then because Uproar (Punk Rock + STAB) comes online at L8 and hits remarkably hard for Rusturf Tunnel.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Punk Rock** — Sound moves deal 1.3x damage; incoming sound moves deal 0.5x. Core identity — every sound move in the kit is boosted.
- **Ability 2: Soundproof** — Immune to sound moves. Defensive option; loses the damage boost.
- **Hidden: Scrappy** — Normal/Fighting moves hit Ghost types. Alt build using Double-Edge and Return for physical coverage.

## The Math

With 105 SpA + Punk Rock (1.3x on sound moves):

| Move | BP | Punk Rock | STAB | Effective BP |
|------|----|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Boomburst** | 140 | ×1.3 = 182 | ×1.5 | **273** |
| **Hyper Voice** | 90 | ×1.3 = 117 | ×1.5 | **175.5** |
| **Uproar** | 90 | ×1.3 = 117 | ×1.5 | **175.5** |
| **Bug Buzz** | 90 | ×1.3 = 117 | — | **117** |

**Choice Specs Boomburst:** 273 eff BP off 157.5 SpA (105 × 1.5). Nearly nothing survives a neutral hit.

**Throat Spray Boomburst:** Fire off Boomburst → Throat Spray triggers (+1 SpA). Now at 157.5 SpA and no item lock. Follow up with coverage moves or fire a second Boomburst at nuke tier.

## Ideal Items

1. **Choice Specs** — 105 SpA × 1.5 = 157.5 effective. Boomburst at 273 eff BP off 157.5 = delete target. Locked into one move, but Boomburst hits everything neutral except Ghost (Scrappy solves that with Focus Blast coverage).
2. **Throat Spray** — One-time +1 SpA proc on a sound move. Use Boomburst or Hyper Voice to trigger it, then attack freely. Rewards prediction over brute force.
3. **Life Orb** — 1.3x damage boost on everything, flexible moveset. Boomburst: 273 eff × 1.3 = 354 eff BP. At a cost. For reckless players.

## Core Strategy

### The Sound Cannon
1. Bring Exploud in on a weak or resisted attacker (80 Speed lets it outspeed much of the mid-game).
2. Boomburst → 273 effective BP. Target either faints or is severely damaged.
3. If they send in a Ghost → Focus Blast for Fighting coverage (or Normal Scrappy if running that).
4. If they send in a Steel/Rock → Focus Blast.
5. Choice Specs locks you in but most teams have no answer to 273 eff STAB.

### Throat Spray Setup
1. Use Hyper Voice or Disarming Voice first turn → Throat Spray triggers (+1 SpA to 157.5).
2. Now free to switch moves. Boomburst at +1 is catastrophic.
3. Bug Buzz on predicted Psychic/Dark switch.

### Early Game (Rusturf Tunnel)
Whismur at L9-11 with Uproar (90 BP, Punk Rock + STAB = 175.5 eff off 60 SpA) is legitimately strong for that point in the game. Trainers in the tunnel and early Dewford gym members get deleted.

## Team Synergy

- Pairs with a Ghost type to cover Normal immunity (Exploud can't hit Ghosts without Scrappy or Fighting coverage)
- Pairs with hazard setters — Boomburst weakens things, teammates clean up
- Coverage partners for Dark/Steel types that tank Focus Blast misses
- U-Turn users to bring Exploud in safely

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Everything that takes neutral damage from Normal. Boomburst off 105+ SpA with Punk Rock is one of the hardest hits in the game.

**Weak vs:**
- Ghost types (Normal immune — bring Fighting coverage or Scrappy)
- Rock types (if they pack Special Defense or hit back hard with Rock Blast)
- Faster threats (80 Speed is good but not elite — Choice Scarf users outpace it)

**Key vulnerability:** Pure mono-Normal coverage is walled by Ghost. Focus Blast and Bug Buzz are essential coverage moves, not luxury picks.

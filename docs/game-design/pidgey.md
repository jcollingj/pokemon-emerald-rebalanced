# Pidgey / Pidgeotto / Pidgeot - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy
Pidgeot is a fast physical sweeper that uses Aerilate to turn Normal moves into boosted Flying STAB. Every Normal move in the kit becomes a Flying-type attack with a 1.2x power boost — Return, Quick Attack, Double-Edge, Facade all become devastating Flying STAB. The "every move benefits" pattern.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Aerilate** — Normal-type moves become Flying-type and get 1.2x power boost. Core identity.
- **Ability 2: Keen Eye** — Kept for flavor. Prevents accuracy drops.
- **Hidden: Big Pecks** — Defense can't be lowered.

## The Math

With 105 Atk + Aerilate (Normal → Flying + 1.2x):

| Move | Base BP | Aerilate BP | STAB (×1.5) | Effective |
|------|---------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Quick Attack | 40 | 48 | 72 | 72, **+1 priority** |
| Return | 102 | 122 | 183 | **183** |
| Double-Edge | 120 | 144 | 216 | **216** (recoil) |
| Extreme Speed | 80 | 96 | 144 | **144, +2 priority** |
| Facade (statused) | 140 | 168 | 252 | **252** |

Aerilate Extreme Speed: 144 effective power at +2 priority. That's a priority Brave Bird without recoil.

## Ideal Items

1. **Silk Scarf** — 1.2x Normal moves... which become Flying via Aerilate. Stacks: 102 × 1.2 (Scarf) × 1.2 (Aerilate) × 1.5 (STAB) = **220 effective Return**.
2. **Choice Band** — Raw 105 Atk × 1.5 = 157.5. Massive hits.
3. **Sharp Beak** — 1.2x Flying moves. Since Aerilate converts to Flying, this boosts the converted moves.

## Core Strategy

1. Fast — 110 Speed outspeeds most of the game
2. Return/Double-Edge for massive Aerilate STAB damage
3. Quick Attack and Extreme Speed for priority — clean up weakened targets
4. U-Turn to pivot out safely
5. Simple, effective, fast. No setup needed.

## Team Synergy

- Fast revenge killer for slower teams
- U-Turn pivoting pairs with Gardevoir's Trace re-entry or Slaking switch-ins
- Covers Fighting/Grass/Bug types that threaten Breloom, Surskit, Lotad

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Fighting, Bug, Grass, Ground (immune via Flying)
**Weak vs:** Electric, Rock, Steel, Ice
**Key move:** Extreme Speed priority means nothing outspeeds Pidgeot in a pinch

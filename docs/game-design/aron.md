# Aron / Lairon / Aggron (#304 / #305 / #306)

## Build Identity

Aggron is a **slow physical wall** that uses **Rock Head** to fire recoil moves for free — Take Down, Double-Edge, and the signature Head Smash (150 BP Rock STAB) deal full damage with zero HP cost.

## Encounter Context

Aron is a rare chase encounter in Rusturf Tunnel (1 of 12 slots, L10–12). Steel/Rock typing makes it immediately distinctive — 10 resistances, but 4x weak to Fighting and Ground.

## Ability

**Rock Head** — Prevents all recoil damage from moves like Take Down, Double-Edge, and Head Smash. Every recoil move in the kit becomes free.

- **Primary:** Rock Head
- **Secondary:** Sturdy (survives a KO hit at full HP — useful with low SpD)
- **Hidden:** Filter (reduces super-effective damage by 25% — partially patches the 4x weaknesses)

- Small Atk bumps across the line keep the damage math exciting at each stage.

## Core Strategy

Aron is immediately usable in Rusturf Tunnel — Take Down at L8 hits for 90 BP with zero HP cost, which is absurd at L10. Evolve into Lairon at L32 and pick up Rock Slide for STAB coverage. Evolve into Aggron at L42 and run Choice Band + Head Smash for late-game nuking. Rock Polish unlocks a surprise +2 Speed sweeper mode that opponents won't see coming from a 50 base Speed Pokemon.

The play pattern is simple but satisfying: switch into a physical attacker that can't break through 180 Def, then fire off Head Smash or Double-Edge at full power with no drawback.

## Team Synergy

- Pairs well with Earthquake users who can cover Aggron's Fighting/Ground weakness
- Works as a physical wall in a defensive core with a Water or Grass type handling Ground threats
- Lures Fighting moves, benefiting teammates that resist Fighting (e.g., Psychic or Flying types)

## Matchups

**Strong against:** Fairy, Flying, Ice, Normal, Poison, Psychic, Rock, Steel, Bug, Dragon — 10 resistances total. Physical attackers largely bounce off 180 Def.

**Weak against:** Fighting (4x), Ground (4x), Water, Fire. Low SpD (even after buff) means special attackers threaten it. Needs teammates to handle these.

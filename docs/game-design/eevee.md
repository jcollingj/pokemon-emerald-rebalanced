# Eevee Line - Rebalance Notes

## Overview

Eevee is a rare encounter on Route 116 (L7-8, slot 11 — ~1% encounter rate). It is the gateway to five distinct Eeveelution builds covering Water, Electric, Fire, Psychic, and Dark types.

The core fantasy: catch a rare Eevee, use it as a setup/support pivot with Baton Pass, then evolve it into the Eeveelution that fits your team's needs.

---

## EEVEE (#133) — Adaptability Baton Pass Setup

**Build Identity:** A versatile support Pokemon that boosts its team with Agility/Bulk Up and passes the boosts via Baton Pass, or goes standalone with Adaptability Swift for solid STAB damage.

### Ability: Adaptability
STAB multiplier increased from 1.5x to 2.0x. Swift becomes 120 effective BP — never-miss Normal nuke for a pre-evolution. But the real fantasy is setting up and passing to an Eeveelution sweeper.

### Eevee Stats

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced | Change |
|------|---------|------------|--------|
| HP | 55 | **70** | +15 |
| Atk | 55 | 55 | — |
| Def | 50 | **60** | +10 |
| SpA | 45 | 45 | — |
| SpD | 65 | **70** | +5 |
| Spe | 55 | **75** | +20 |
| **BST** | **325** | **375** | **+50** |

**Rationale:** Not a damage dealer. Speed and bulk allow it to survive long enough to set up and Baton Pass. 75 Speed outruns most early Route 116 threats.

### Eevee Learnset

| Level | Move | Type | Purpose |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| 1 | Tackle | Normal | Basic attack |
| 1 | Growl | Normal | Defense debuff utility |
| 8 | **Swift** | Normal | STAB, never misses — Adaptability = 120 eff. BP |
| 10 | **Wish** | Normal | Self-heal or pass healing to team |
| 12 | **Agility** | Psychic | +2 Speed to pass to a sweeper |
| 13 | **Bulk Up** | Fighting | +1 Atk/Def to pass |
| 14 | **Baton Pass** | Normal | The signature — pass all boosts to an Eeveelution |

### Core Strategy
1. Use Agility (+2 Spe) and/or Bulk Up (+1 Atk/Def)
2. Baton Pass to a Jolteon/Vaporeon/Flareon/Espeon/Umbreon
3. The receiver sweeps with boosted stats

Alternative: Use Eevee standalone with Adaptability Swift for a never-miss 120 BP Normal move while it's still leveling.

### Ideal Items
- **Salac Berry** — Boosts Speed at low HP, then Baton Pass for a double speed boost
- **Sitrus Berry** — Survivability to set up more boosts before passing
- **Lum Berry** — Protection from status while setting up

## VAPOREON (#134) — Water Absorb Tank

**Build Identity:** A bulky Water-type tank that heals with Water Absorb and Aqua Ring, walls physical threats with Acid Armor, and dishes out strong Water STAB early.

### Ability: Water Absorb
Immune to Water moves, restores 1/4 HP. With Aqua Ring (1/16 per turn) + Water Absorb, Vaporeon is a self-sustaining wall against Water types.

## FLAREON (#136) — Guts Physical Fire Attacker

**Build Identity:** A physical Fire attacker that uses Guts + Flame Orb to turn status into a 1.5x Attack multiplier, then nukes with Flare Blitz and Superpower.

### Abilities
- **Ability 1: Guts** — 1.5x Attack when statused. Flame Orb: 130 Atk × 1.5 Guts = 195 effective Attack
- **Ability 2: Flash Fire** — Immune to Fire, boosts Fire moves if hit
- **Hidden: Reckless** — +20% damage on recoil moves (Flare Blitz becomes even stronger)

### Flareon Stats

| Stat | Vanilla | Rebalanced | Change |
|------|---------|------------|--------|
| HP | 65 | 65 | — |
| Atk | 130 | 130 | — |
| Def | 60 | 60 | — |
| SpA | 95 | 95 | — |
| SpD | 110 | 110 | — |
| Spe | 65 | **80** | +15 |
| **BST** | **525** | **540** | **+15** |

**Rationale:** Speed was the bottleneck. 80 Speed lets it outspeed more threats without a Choice Scarf.

### Flareon Learnset

| Level | Move | Type | Purpose |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| 1 | Tackle | Normal | Basic attack |
| 1 | Tail Whip | Normal | Defense debuff |
| 1 | Ember | Fire | Basic Fire STAB |
| 8 | **Fire Fang** | Fire | Physical Fire STAB — build turns on |
| 16 | Bite | Dark | Physical coverage vs Psychic/Ghost |
| 24 | **Flare Blitz** | Fire | 120 BP recoil Fire STAB — core move |
| 27 | Will-O-Wisp | Fire | Status utility (synergy with Guts on self via Flame Orb) |
| 30 | **Superpower** | Fighting | Coverage vs Normal/Rock/Ice/Steel |
| 36 | Leer | Normal | Defense debuff utility |
| 42 | Flamethrower | Fire | Special Fire option |
| 48 | Fire Blast | Fire | Maximum power Fire STAB |

### Core Strategy
1. Equip Flame Orb — burn activates Guts (+50% Attack)
2. Flare Blitz: 120 BP × 1.5 Guts = 180 effective BP at 130 Atk
3. Reckless (hidden): Flare Blitz becomes 144 effective BP before Guts
4. Superpower for Normal/Rock/Ice/Steel coverage
5. Guts also neutralizes the Attack drop from Superpower being relevant (Atk drop is still there but Guts keeps it high)

### Ideal Items
- **Flame Orb** — Activates Guts, makes Flareon a physical monster
- **Choice Band** — For players without Flame Orb access; 130 × 1.5 = 195 Atk
- **Life Orb** — Flexible moveset with 1.3x damage boost

## Encounter Location

**Route 116** — Rare encounter (slot 11, ~1% encounter rate)
- Level range: 7-8
- Replaces one Skitty slot
- Evolves via:
  - Water Stone → Vaporeon
  - Thunder Stone → Jolteon
  - Fire Stone → Flareon
  - High friendship (day) → Espeon
  - High friendship (night) → Umbreon

# Wingull / Pelipper - Rebalance Notes

## Design Philosophy
Pelipper is the rain team's engine. Drizzle sets rain on switch-in — enabling every rain sweeper in the game (Poliwag's Swift Swim, Surskit's Swift Swim, Mudkip's Rain Dish). Pelipper doesn't need to be a star attacker — it needs to set rain, take hits, pivot out, and come back to reset rain later. Bulky support.

## Ability Changes

- **Ability 1: Drizzle** — Sets rain for 5 turns on switch-in (8 with Damp Rock). Core identity.
- **Ability 2: Keen Eye** — Accuracy can't be lowered.
- **Hidden: Rain Dish** — Heals 1/8 HP in rain. Sustain option that synergizes with its own Drizzle.

## Ideal Items

1. **Damp Rock** — Rain lasts 8 turns instead of 5. More rain = more time for Swift Swim sweepers. Available on Route 104 north.
2. **Leftovers** — Sustain for repeated switch-ins to reset rain.
3. **Sitrus Berry** — Instant heal on switch-in, good for early game.

## Core Strategy

### Rain Setter Pivot
1. Switch Pelipper in → Drizzle sets rain (8 turns with Damp Rock)
2. Scald for Burns + rain-boosted damage, or Hurricane for big Flying STAB
3. U-Turn out → bring in Swift Swim sweeper (Poliwag, Surskit) to sweep in rain
4. When rain ends, switch Pelipper back in → Drizzle resets rain
5. Repeat the cycle

### The Rain Team Core
- **Pelipper** sets rain → U-Turns out
- **Poliwag/Poliwrath** (Swift Swim) doubles Speed in rain, sweeps
- **Surskit/Masquerain** (Swift Swim) doubles Speed, Tail Glow + sweep
- **Mudkip/Swampert** (Rain Dish) heals 1/8 HP + 1.5x Def in rain, tanks
- **Lotad/Ludicolo** (Rain Dish) rain support

## Team Synergy

- THE rain enabler. Every rain Pokemon in the game depends on Pelipper.
- U-Turn pivoting brings rain sweepers in safely
- Scald burns cripple physical attackers for the whole team
- Hurricane + rain = 165 effective never-miss STAB
- Tailwind boosts team speed even without rain

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Fire (rain weakens Fire moves + Water STAB), Ground, Rock, Steel
**Weak vs:** Electric (4x!), Rock. Grass resists Water moves.
**Key vulnerability:** Electric types are devastating — 4x weakness. Keep away from Pikachu.

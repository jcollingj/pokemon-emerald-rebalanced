# Abra / Kadabra / Alakazam — Rebalance Notes

## Build Identity

Alakazam is a nuclear special sweeper that uses **Magic Guard** to run Life Orb with zero recoil, turning 135 SpA into 175 effective Special Attack from the moment it holds the item. It hits everything hard, dies to anything physical, and doesn't care about hazards, weather, or status chip.

---

## Ability: Magic Guard

**Effect:** No indirect damage — Life Orb recoil, entry hazards, weather, burn/poison chip, Leech Seed, etc. all do nothing.

**Why Magic Guard?**
- Life Orb with no recoil is the core build: 135 SpA × 1.3 = **175 effective SpA** with no downside
- Hazard immunity means Alakazam can switch in freely despite paper defenses
- Status immunity (to chip) means Toxic/Burn can't wear it down — the only threat is a direct KO
- Sets up Calm Mind safely: opponents can't chip it out during setup turns

Magic Guard is what transforms Alakazam from "fragile glass cannon" to "fragile glass cannon that plays forever as long as nothing directly hits it." The tension is all about surviving physical hits — everything else is nullified.

**Ability 2:** Inner Focus (flinch immunity — lets Alakazam move freely without flinch disruption)
**Hidden:** Synchronize (reflects status — niche but thematic)

## Core Strategy

1. **Switch in** — Magic Guard ignores hazards, no chip damage on entry
2. **Life Orb equipped** — every hit does 30% more damage with zero recoil
3. **Calm Mind** if there's a free turn — 135 SpA + Nasty Plot/Calm Mind becomes absurd
4. **Coverage rotation** — Psychic (STAB), Shadow Ball (Ghost), Dazzling Gleam (Dragon/Dark), Focus Blast (Steel/Dark)
5. **Recover** if at low HP — Magic Guard means only direct attacks matter, so Recover buys many more turns

The build loop: Magic Guard blocks all passive damage → Life Orb is free → switch in repeatedly → use coverage or set up → KO threats before they KO you. Alakazam's weakness is exclusively physical revenge killers — if nothing physical can touch it, it snowballs.

**The tension:** 55 HP / 45 Def means any physical priority move (Mach Punch, Extreme Speed, Bullet Punch) or fast physical attacker ends Alakazam immediately. This is pure boom-or-bust offense.

---

## Team Synergy

- **Pairs with physical walls** (Swampert, Golem) that absorb priority hits and physical coverage Alakazam can't take
- **Pairs with U-Turn/Volt Switch** pivots that bring Alakazam in safely without eating a hit
- **Pairs with Stealth Rock setters** — Alakazam doesn't care about SR itself (Magic Guard), but opponents do
- **Gardevoir comparison:** Alakazam is faster (120 vs 80/85) and has a simpler engine (pure damage). Gardevoir pivots for SpA stacks; Alakazam just hits hard immediately. Different roles despite both being Psychic special attackers.

---

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:**
- Poison, Fighting, Psychic (resists and outspeeds most)
- Anything slower — Alakazam at 120 Speed outspeeds nearly all non-Choice Scarf threats
- Passive walls — Life Orb damage + coverage breaks most defensive cores

**Weak vs:**
- Physical priority moves (Mach Punch, Bullet Punch, Extreme Speed) — OHKO through Speed advantage
- Fast physical attackers (Aerodactyl, Electrode) — 45 Def doesn't survive
- Dark types (Ghost-typing covers Ghosts, but Dark hits Alakazam's SpD... wait, Dark is super-effective vs Psychic)
- Pursuit trappers — can't escape with Teleport while trapped

**Key interaction:** Magic Guard means Toxic/Burn strategies don't work against Alakazam. Opponents must KO it directly.

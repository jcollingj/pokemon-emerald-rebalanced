# Heracross #214 — Rebalance Notes

## Build Identity

Heracross is a **Guts berserker** that uses a Flame Orb to simultaneously activate Guts (+50% Atk) and supercharge Facade (70 → 140 effective BP) — giving it the hardest-hitting Normal move in the game while Megahorn and Close Combat cover Bug and Fighting STAB respectively.

## Design Context

Heracross appears on Brawly's team in Segment 2 (Dewford Gym), where **Brawly's version uses Moxie** — gaining attack with every KO. The **player's version uses Guts** — constant berserker power after Flame Orb activates. Two Heracross, two distinct identities.

---

## Build Options (All Three)

### Option A: Moxie — KO Snowballer

**Ability:** Moxie — +1 Atk on every KO.

Megahorn + Moxie + Life Orb: first kill starts the avalanche. Used by Brawly's Heracross, so this lives as the Hidden Ability option for players who want the trainer-mirror experience.

**Pros:** Explosive mid-battle momentum. Rewarding skill expression — only gains if you get the KO.
**Cons:** Passive if KOs are missed. Thematically adjacent to Nidoking's "one activation unlocks hell" feel.

---

### Option B: Guts — Iron Will Berserker ✅ RECOMMENDED (Ability 1)

**Ability:** Guts — +50% Atk when statused, burn damage penalty on physical moves ignored.

The **Flame Orb double-synergy** build: one item simultaneously activates Guts AND transforms Facade from 70 BP → **140 effective BP**. Effective Atk becomes 187.5 × Facade 140 = the hardest-hitting Normal move in the game. Megahorn handles Psychic coverage; Close Combat handles everything else.

**Why recommended:**
- Facade + Guts is completely unique in the roster — no other Pokemon uses this
- Brawly already runs Moxie Heracross, so the player's version has a distinct identity
- "Endure pain to hit harder" is beetle-warrior flavor done right
- Consistent power (always on after Flame Orb) vs Moxie's conditional snowball

**Cons:** Flame Orb has to drop first; vulnerable before item activates.

---

### Option C: Reckless — The Crashing Beetle (Unusual Pick)

**Ability:** Reckless (reworked from Swarm) — +20% BP on recoil/crash moves.

High Jump Kick + Reckless = **156 eff BP Fighting STAB** — the highest single-hit Fighting move available. Thematic for a charging horn beetle.

**Cons:** HJK misses = 50% crash damage. High-variance playstyle that's frustrating in single-player. Makes Megahorn the passive move rather than the signature.

## The Math

With 125 Atk × Guts 1.5× when statused:
- **Effective Atk:** 187.5
- **Facade (Guts):** 70 × 2 × 1.5 = **210 eff** (Normal, hits everything not a Ghost)
- **Megahorn:** 120 × 1.5 = **180 eff Bug STAB** — one-shots Psychic types
- **Close Combat:** 120 × 1.5 = **180 eff Fighting STAB** — nukes Steel, Normal, Rock, Dark, Ice

With Flame Orb + Choice Band stacked: Facade hits 140 × 1.5 × 1.5 = **315 eff BP**. That's effectively a 315 BP Normal move on any non-Ghost.

---

## Core Strategy

1. Switch in and activate **Flame Orb** — takes 1 turn of burn chip to proc Guts
2. **Facade** is now the primary attack — 210 eff BP, hits everything but Ghosts
3. **Megahorn** for Psychic types (the primary threat to Bug/Fighting)
4. **Close Combat** for bulky Normal, Rock, Dark, Steel switchins
5. **Endure → Reversal** as a clutch reversal option when burn chip brings HP low

## Team Synergy

- Pairs with a cleric or Aromatherapy user if you want to cure burn mid-battle (though you usually want it active)
- Pairs well with Psychic-weak teammates — Heracross removes Psychics with Megahorn
- Knock Off users on the team support by removing defensive items before Heracross sweeps
- Fast pivot (U-turn/Volt Switch) users can get Heracross in safely to start the burn clock

---

## Matchup Analysis

**Strong vs:** Psychic (Megahorn), Normal, Dark, Rock, Steel, Ice (Close Combat), Ghost-less teams (Facade)
**Weak vs:** Flying (2×), Fire (2×), Psychic (4×) — Megahorn pre-empts Psychic but Heracross dies to Psychic hits
**Key threats:** Ghost types wall Facade entirely. Fire types hit a 2× weakness and resist Megahorn. Flying types force Heracross out before Facade's momentum builds.

# Billy (Youngster) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Zigzagoon | 8 | None | Pickup | Tackle, Growl, Sand Attack, Headbutt |
| Taillow | 8 | None | Guts | Pluck, Wing Attack, Growl, Quick Attack |
| Poochyena | 9 | None | Guts | Bite, Quick Attack, Scary Face, Tackle |

## Puzzle
Billy is a straightforward brawler team with no frills — the puzzle is managing chip damage from priority moves. All three Pokemon have Quick Attack, so the player takes priority hits every turn. Poochyena is the closer with Scary Face to halve the player's Speed, then Bite for flinch chances. The player learns that stacking priority across a team creates consistent pressure even without big damage numbers.

## Design Notes
- Billy is the simplest Route 104 fight, meant as a warm-up
- All Pokemon are from the local encounter pool (Route 104 Taillow/Poochyena, Route 102 Zigzagoon)
- Guts on both Taillow and Poochyena means status moves are risky — burning or poisoning them makes them hit harder
- No items or complex synergies — just solid movesets that teach priority mechanics
- Scary Face on Poochyena forces a speed disadvantage the player has to play around

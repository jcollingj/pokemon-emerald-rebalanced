# Josh (Youngster) — Rustboro Gym

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Geodude | 10 | None | Stamina | Defense Curl, Rollout, Rock Throw, Tackle |
| Larvitar | 10 | None | Sand Stream | Rock Slide, Bite, Leer, Tackle |
| Aron | 11 | None | Heavy Metal | Tackle, Harden, Metal Claw, Headbutt |

## Puzzle
Josh is the gym intro fight, teaching the player what Roxanne's gym is about: Rock types with defensive abilities. Geodude opens with the Stamina + Defense Curl + Rollout engine the player may have seen on Route 116 — if they haven't, this is the learning moment. Larvitar brings Sand Stream on entry, summoning sandstorm that boosts all Rock-type SpDef by 50% and chips non-Rock player Pokemon with residual damage. Aron with Heavy Metal + Harden previews the armored titan identity. The player learns: use Water, Grass, or Fighting to break through.

## Design Notes
- Josh is the first gym trainer and the easiest — a stepping stone to Tommy and Marc
- Geodude at L10 with Stamina is the standard Rollout threat the player needs to solve
- Larvitar at L10 sets sandstorm, which makes Geodude and Aron's SpDef effectively 50% higher
- Sand Stream is the mechanic Roxanne's own Larvitar uses — Josh previews it
- Aron from Rusturf Tunnel is rare in the wild (1 slot) — the player sees it here first
- No items on any Pokemon keeps the intro fight manageable
- Rock Slide, Rock Throw, and Tackle give the player a chance to see Rock STAB without getting overwhelmed

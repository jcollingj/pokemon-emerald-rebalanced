# Tommy (Youngster) — Rustboro Gym

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Geodude | 11 | None | Stamina | Defense Curl, Rollout, Bulldoze, Rock Throw |
| Omanyte | 11 | None | Swift Swim | Water Gun, Ancient Power, Bite, Withdraw |
| Larvitar | 12 | None | Sand Stream | Rock Slide, Crunch, Stealth Rock, Leer |

## Puzzle
Tommy escalates the gym with a fossil Pokemon and sandstorm hazards. Geodude with Stamina + Rollout is the familiar threat. Omanyte is the surprise — it's a Water/Rock type with Swift Swim, meaning if the player uses Rain Dance to counter sandstorm, Omanyte doubles its Speed and Water Gun gets rain-boosted. The player can't just weather-counter the gym. Larvitar sets Stealth Rock for chip damage on switches, and Sand Stream resets sandstorm. The player faces a team that punishes both staying in (Rollout snowball) and switching (Stealth Rock chip + sand damage).

## Design Notes
- Tommy is the mid-tier gym trainer, harder than Josh but easier than Marc
- Omanyte is from Roxanne's own team — the player sees it here first as a preview
- Swift Swim on Omanyte is an anti-rain tech: players who bring Damp Rock + Rain Dance get punished
- Larvitar at L12 with Stealth Rock is the first time the player faces entry hazards in a gym context
- Bulldoze on Geodude adds Ground coverage and slows the player's Pokemon on hit
- Crunch on Larvitar is Dark STAB coverage for Psychic and Ghost types that resist Rock
- The team covers Rock, Water, Dark, and Ground — the player needs diverse counterplay

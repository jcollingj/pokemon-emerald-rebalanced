# Tiana (Lass) — Route 102

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |

## Puzzle
Tiana's team has deceptive damage output. Zigzagoon's Pickup means the first hit deals half damage (Multiscale at full HP), baiting the player into thinking it's weak — then Headbutt flinches. Shroomish has Technician Mach Punch (+1 priority) and Bullet Seed, so it hits before the player and multi-hit breaks through. Marill's Huge Power doubles its Attack, making Aqua Jet surprisingly strong priority Water STAB. The player faces three sources of priority damage.

## Design Notes
- Tiana is the Route 102 priority lesson — Mach Punch, Aqua Jet, and Zigzagoon's flinch-chance Headbutt all punish slow play
- Zigzagoon's Pickup Multiscale at full HP teaches the mechanic early: first hit does half, then it hits back
- Lotad is the "slow and annoying" anchor with Leech Seed drain, forcing the player to either KO it fast or watch their HP tick away
- 4 Pokemon gives Tiana the biggest team on Route 102, making her the hardest optional fight here
- Shroomish with Technician Mach Punch at L8 previews Breloom's identity early

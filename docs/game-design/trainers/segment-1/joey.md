# Joey (Youngster) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Machop | 9 | None | Guts | Low Kick, Leer, Focus Energy, Karate Chop |
| Poochyena | 8 | None | Guts | Bite, Quick Attack, Scary Face, Tackle |
| Marill | 8 | None | Huge Power | Aqua Jet, Water Gun, Tackle, Defense Curl |

## Puzzle
Joey leads with the only Machop on Route 104 — a Fighting type the player likely hasn't encountered yet. Focus Energy + Karate Chop gives high crit rate, and Low Kick threatens heavier Pokemon. Poochyena's Dark STAB covers the Psychic types that might counter Machop. Marill's Huge Power Aqua Jet is priority cleanup. The puzzle: Machop forces the player to find a Flying or Psychic answer, but Poochyena punishes Psychic types with Bite.

## Design Notes
- Machop is a trainer-only Pokemon not in the wild pool, making Joey's team feel unique
- Guts on both Machop and Poochyena means the player can't use status to weaken the team
- Fighting + Dark + Water gives Joey good type coverage for three Pokemon
- Marill's Huge Power makes its 30 base Atk into 60 effective — Aqua Jet hits harder than expected
- Focus Energy + Karate Chop on Machop previews crit-based play early

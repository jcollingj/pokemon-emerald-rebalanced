# Devan (Hiker) — Route 116

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Geodude | 9 | None | Stamina | Defense Curl, Rollout, Bulldoze, Tackle |
| Nincada | 9 | None | Speed Boost | Scratch, Sand Attack, Mud Slap, Harden |
| Makuhita | 10 | None | Iron Fist | Mach Punch, Fire Punch, Fake Out, Tackle |

## Puzzle
Devan adds variety with Nincada alongside the expected rock types. Geodude opens with the Defense Curl + Rollout engine, but Nincada is the surprise — Speed Boost makes it faster each turn while Sand Attack and Mud Slap stack accuracy drops. If the player doesn't KO Nincada quickly, they'll be fighting at -2 or -3 accuracy while it outspeeds them. Makuhita closes with Iron Fist Fire Punch (90 effective BP) covering the Grass types the player might bring for Geodude.

## Design Notes
- Devan is differentiated from Clark by including Nincada — a Route 116 native that's unusual for a Hiker
- Nincada's Speed Boost + accuracy drops create a timer: the longer the fight goes, the harder it gets
- Makuhita with Fire Punch specifically punishes Shroomish/Lotad that the player might lean on for Geodude
- This teaches the player that Hikers can have unexpected coverage and aren't just "Geodude spam"
- 90 Def Nincada is surprisingly tanky physically — the player might be surprised it doesn't go down quickly

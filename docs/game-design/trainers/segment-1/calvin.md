## Puzzle
Calvin teaches the player about Dark-type priority. Both Poochyena and Rattata carry Quick Attack and Bite, so the player faces consistent chip damage from priority moves and flinch-chance Dark STAB. Nidoran M anchors with physical Poison coverage, threatening a poison status on contact.

## Design Notes
- Calvin is the first trainer on Route 102 and should be a gentle introduction
- Poochyena and Rattata share Strong Jaw / Guts identity and both have Bite, but Poochyena is bulkier (85 HP) while Rattata is faster (75 Spe) — the player sees two different takes on similar moves
- Nidoran M introduces the poison threat that defines Route 102's encounter pool
- No items keeps the fight readable for early game
- Differentiated from Allen by focusing on Dark/Poison physical attackers rather than Normal/Flying types

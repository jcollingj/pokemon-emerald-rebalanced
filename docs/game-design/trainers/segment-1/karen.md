# Karen (School Kid) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Shroomish | 9 | None | Technician | Bullet Seed, Mach Punch, Stun Spore, Absorb |
| Wurmple | 8 | None | Technician | Pin Missile, Poison Sting, Tackle, String Shot |
| Marill | 9 | None | Huge Power | Aqua Jet, Water Gun, Play Rough, Defense Curl |

## Puzzle
Karen doubles down on the Technician theme. Both Shroomish and Wurmple use Technician to boost weak multi-hit moves — Bullet Seed and Pin Missile hit 2-5 times with each hit boosted by 50%. The player sees the same ability on two different Pokemon, reinforcing how Technician works. Marill is the Huge Power closer with priority Aqua Jet. The puzzle is managing multi-hit damage that varies wildly between 2 and 5 hits.

## Design Notes
- Karen is the Technician teacher — two Technician users help the player understand the ability
- Shroomish's Mach Punch (priority Fighting STAB, 60 effective with Technician) is the real threat
- Stun Spore from Shroomish paralyzes the player's Pokemon, making Marill's Aqua Jet even harder to outrun
- Wurmple with Pin Missile is a callback to Rick on Route 102 — the player sees Technician bugs again
- Play Rough on Marill adds Fairy coverage the player might not expect from a Water type
- A School Kid running Technician-themed Pokemon fits the "theory nerd" archetype

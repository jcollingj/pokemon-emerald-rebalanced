# Janice (Lass) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Marill | 9 | None | Huge Power | Aqua Jet, Play Rough, Water Gun, Defense Curl |
| Wingull | 8 | None | Drizzle | Water Gun, Wing Attack, Growl, Water Pulse |
| Surskit | 8 | None | Swift Swim | Water Gun, Silver Wind, Quick Attack, Bubble |

## Puzzle
Janice runs a proto-rain team. Wingull sets Drizzle on entry, which activates Surskit's Swift Swim (65 Spe doubles to 130 in rain) and boosts all Water moves by 1.5x. Marill's Huge Power Aqua Jet in rain becomes 40 BP x 2 (HP) x 1.5 (STAB) x 1.5 (rain) = 180 effective damage off priority. The player learns that rain makes Water types dramatically stronger and faster.

## Design Notes
- Janice introduces the rain team concept earlier than Route 103 trainers (lower level, simpler execution)
- Wingull as lead is a deliberate Drizzle showcase — the player sees rain start immediately
- Surskit with Swift Swim in rain outspeeds most of the player's team at this point — Silver Wind is Bug STAB coverage
- Marill's Play Rough at L9 is unusual — teaches the player about Fairy coverage early
- All three are from the Route 104 encounter pool
- The counterplay is clear: Grass types resist Water and hit back super effectively against all three

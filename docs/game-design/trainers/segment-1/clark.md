## Puzzle
Clark runs the classic Hiker rock/cave setup. His lead Geodude uses the Stamina + Defense Curl + Rollout combo — each physical hit the player lands makes Geodude tankier via Stamina, and Defense Curl doubles Rollout's power. If the player doesn't KO it fast with a special Water/Grass move, Rollout snowballs. The second Geodude uses Sturdy to guarantee surviving one hit, then fires Rock Throw and Bulldoze. Makuhita closes with Iron Fist Mach Punch priority and Fake Out for a free flinch turn.

## Design Notes
- Clark is the introduction to Geodude's Stamina + Rollout build that Roxanne uses
- Two Geodude with different abilities (Stamina vs Sturdy) shows the player both options
- The first Geodude is the real test — the player learns to use special moves or super effective attacks to bypass the Defense Curl + Stamina loop
- Makuhita from Rusturf Tunnel is thematically appropriate for a Hiker
- Iron Fist Mach Punch at 48 effective BP with priority ensures Makuhita always gets a hit in
- Fake Out on turn 1 steals the player's first action — forces them to take damage before they can respond

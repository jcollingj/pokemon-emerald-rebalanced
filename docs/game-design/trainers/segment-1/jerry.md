# Jerry (School Kid) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Ralts | 9 | None | Trace | Confusion, Disarming Voice, Growl, Calm Mind |
| Wingull | 8 | None | Drizzle | Water Gun, Wing Attack, Supersonic, Growl |
| Taillow | 9 | None | Guts | Wing Attack, Pluck, Quick Attack, Growl |

## Puzzle
Jerry is the "smart kid" who leads with Ralts and a Calm Mind setup. If the player doesn't pressure Ralts immediately, it boosts SpA and SpD, then fires off Confusion and Disarming Voice with increasing power. Trace copies the player's ability, which can create surprising interactions. Wingull brings Drizzle weather pressure, and Taillow is a Guts tank that punishes status moves. The player learns to pressure setup Pokemon early.

## Design Notes
- Jerry's team is built around answering different player strategies: setup (Ralts), weather (Wingull), and status punishment (Taillow/Guts)
- Ralts with Calm Mind at L9 is a teaching moment — the player needs to learn to KO setup sweepers before they boost
- Trace on Ralts creates unique moments: copying Huge Power on a switch, or Technician, etc.
- Taillow's Guts + 70/70/70 bulk means it's surprisingly hard to KO, especially if the player tries to burn/poison it
- A School Kid running Ralts thematically fits — the "studious" trainer uses the Psychic type

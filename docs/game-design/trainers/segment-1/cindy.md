# Cindy (Lady) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Zigzagoon | 9 | Nugget | Pickup | Tackle, Headbutt, Sand Attack, Growl |
| Ralts | 9 | None | Trace | Confusion, Disarming Voice, Growl, Calm Mind |
| Skitty | 9 | None | Normalize | Fake Out, Tackle, Sing, Growl |

## Puzzle
Cindy pairs money (Nugget on Zigzagoon) with status disruption. Zigzagoon's Pickup Multiscale tanks the first hit, Sand Attack drops accuracy. Ralts sets up with Calm Mind and attacks with dual Psychic/Fairy STAB. Skitty is the surprise — Normalize turns Fake Out into a priority Normal STAB flinch, and Sing can put the player's Pokemon to sleep. The player is rewarded with money but has to earn it through accuracy drops and sleep threats.

## Design Notes
- Cindy is a money trainer (Nugget = 5000 reward) — the player is incentivized to fight her
- Zigzagoon with Nugget is thematic for a Lady trainer and for Pickup's item-finding identity
- Ralts with Calm Mind at L9 previews the setup-and-sweep identity of Gardevoir
- Skitty with Normalize at L9 is a Route 116 Pokemon appearing early on a trainer — previews the ability
- Fake Out (priority flinch) + Sing (sleep) makes Skitty a disruptive closer
- Paired with Winston as the Rich Boy/Lady duo

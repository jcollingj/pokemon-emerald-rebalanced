# Allen (Youngster) — Route 102

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Taillow | 7 | None | Guts | Pluck, Wing Attack, Growl, Quick Attack |
| Pidgey | 7 | None | Aerilate | Tackle, Quick Attack, Sand Attack, Gust |
| Zigzagoon | 8 | None | Pickup | Tackle, Growl, Sand Attack, Headbutt |

## Puzzle
Allen is the "bird boy" who teaches type matchups through two different Flying-type builds. Taillow's Guts makes it surprisingly bulky (70/70/70) and its Wing Attack is STAB chip, while Pidgey's Aerilate turns Tackle and Quick Attack into Flying-type moves with a 1.2x boost. The player sees the same type (Normal/Flying) executed in two opposite ways — one defensive, one offensive. Zigzagoon anchors with Pickup's Multiscale-at-full-HP to close out.

## Design Notes
- Differentiated from Calvin: Allen is Normal/Flying themed, Calvin is Dark/Poison themed
- Taillow with Guts + 70/70/70 bulk is the "weird tank bird" — the player expects a frail bird and gets something that takes hits
- Pidgey with Aerilate shows how an ability transforms Normal moves into Flying STAB — Quick Attack becomes 48 effective Flying priority
- Zigzagoon as closer uses Pickup Multiscale to tank the first hit, teaching the mechanic if the player hasn't seen Calvin's team
- Sand Attack on both Pidgey and Zigzagoon means accuracy drops stack if the player doesn't switch

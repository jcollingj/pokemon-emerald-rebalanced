# Haley (Lass) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Lotad | 8 | None | Rain Dish | Absorb, Leech Seed, Water Gun, Growl |
| Shroomish | 9 | None | Technician | Bullet Seed, Mach Punch, Absorb, Stun Spore |
| Ralts | 9 | None | Trace | Confusion, Disarming Voice, Growl, Psybeam |

## Puzzle
Haley runs a drain-and-status team. Lotad opens with Leech Seed to start passive HP loss, then Shroomish comes in with Stun Spore to paralyze — now the player is losing HP from Leech Seed, slowed by paralysis, and eating Technician Mach Punch priority. Ralts is the special attacker closer with Trace (copies the opponent's ability) and dual STAB in Psychic + Fairy. The player needs to break the drain loop before it overwhelms them.

## Design Notes
- Haley's team draws from Petalburg Woods and Route 102 Pokemon the player could have caught nearby
- Lotad's Leech Seed + Rain Dish previews the drain-stall identity early (no rain up, but the threat is visible)
- Shroomish's Technician Mach Punch at L9 hits at 60 effective BP with priority — surprisingly strong
- Ralts with Trace copies the player's own ability, creating a mirror moment that teaches what Trace does
- Psybeam on Ralts at L9 gives real Psychic STAB (65 BP) — with 55 rebalanced SpA, it hurts
- The Grass/Grass/Psychic typing means Flying and Fire types clean this team — clear counterplay

# Winston (Rich Boy) — Route 104

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Zigzagoon | 9 | Nugget | Pickup | Tackle, Headbutt, Sand Attack, Growl |
| Poochyena | 9 | None | Guts | Bite, Quick Attack, Howl, Tackle |
| Wingull | 8 | None | Drizzle | Water Gun, Wing Attack, Supersonic, Growl |

## Puzzle
Winston pairs money with offensive momentum. Zigzagoon's Pickup Multiscale survives the first hit, then Headbutt threatens flinch. Poochyena uses Howl to boost Attack, then Bite for STAB flinch-chance damage — and Guts means trying to status it backfires. Wingull brings Drizzle on entry, boosting its Water Gun and threatening confusion with Supersonic. The player faces flinch chances, confusion, and an Attack-boosted Dark type.

## Design Notes
- Winston is Cindy's counterpart — both carry Nugget Zigzagoon but have different team compositions
- Cindy is status/disruption focused (Sing, Calm Mind); Winston is offense focused (Howl, Bite, Drizzle)
- Poochyena with Howl + Bite is a mini setup sweeper — if the player lets Howl go off, Bite hits hard
- Wingull's Drizzle doesn't benefit the rest of the team much, but teaches the player about weather
- Guts on Poochyena is a subtle trap: burning or poisoning it increases its Attack by 50%

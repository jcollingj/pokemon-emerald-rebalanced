# Darian (Fisherman) — Route 116

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Magikarp | 9 | None | Moxie | Tackle, Aqua Jet, Flail, Splash |
| Tentacool | 9 | None | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Poison Sting, Water Gun, Acid Spray |
| Horsea | 10 | None | Sniper | Water Gun, Smokescreen, Bubble, Leer |

## Puzzle
Darian's team looks like a pushover — it starts with Magikarp. But Moxie Aqua Jet at 50 base Atk is real priority damage, and if Magikarp KOs a weakened target, Moxie triggers and the next Aqua Jet hits harder. Tentacool traps with Whirlpool and stacks Acid Spray (-2 SpDef), then Poison Heal keeps it alive without items. Horsea is the sniper with Smokescreen accuracy drops and Sniper-boosted crits. The player learns not to underestimate a Fisherman's team.

## Design Notes
- Darian brings Water types to Route 116 where they're not naturally found — Fisherman thematic
- Magikarp with Moxie + Aqua Jet is the rebalanced Magikarp identity — no longer useless
- Tentacool's Whirlpool trap + Acid Spray combo previews the Poison Heal trapper identity without Toxic Orb
- Horsea's Sniper ability gives passive +1 crit stage — Water Gun crits hit at 2.25x damage
- Smokescreen on Horsea stacks with any accuracy drops from earlier Pokemon
- The team escalates: joke fish, annoying trapper, crit sniper — each harder than the last

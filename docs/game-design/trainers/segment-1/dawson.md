# Dawson (Rich Boy) — Route 116

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Zigzagoon | 10 | Nugget | Pickup | Headbutt, Tackle, Sand Attack, Growl |
| Poochyena | 10 | None | Guts | Bite, Quick Attack, Howl, Scary Face |
| Abra | 10 | None | Magic Guard | Confusion, Calm Mind, Teleport, Kinesis |

## Puzzle
Dawson is the Route 116 money trainer with a surprise in Abra. Zigzagoon's Pickup Multiscale tanks the first hit, and Headbutt threatens flinch. Poochyena uses Howl + Bite for boosted Dark STAB flinch pressure. But Abra is the real threat: Magic Guard means no passive damage affects it, and Confusion off 105 base SpA at L10 is absurdly strong for this level range. Calm Mind makes it even scarier. The player must KO Abra fast (20 HP/15 Def — it dies to any physical hit) or watch it boost to dangerous levels.

## Design Notes
- Dawson mirrors Route 104's Winston (Nugget Zigzagoon + Poochyena) but adds Abra as an escalation
- Abra with 105 base SpA at L10 is the hardest-hitting Pokemon on any Route 116 trainer
- Magic Guard means the player can't chip Abra with residual damage — direct attacks only
- The glass cannon tension: Abra has 40 rebalanced HP and 20 Def, so any physical hit likely OHKOs it
- But if the player leads with something slow, Abra's 90 Speed + Confusion will chunk them hard
- Nugget reward incentivizes the player to fight Dawson despite the challenge

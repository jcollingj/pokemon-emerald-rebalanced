# Marc (Hiker) — Rustboro Gym

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Larvitar | 12 | Smooth Rock | Sand Stream | Stealth Rock, Rock Slide, Crunch, Protect |
| Geodude | 12 | Wide Lens | Stamina | Defense Curl, Rollout, Rock Throw, Bulldoze |
| Nosepass | 13 | Leftovers | Sand Force | Power Gem, Thunder Wave, Rock Throw, Tackle |

## Puzzle
Marc is the hardest gym trainer and a direct preview of Roxanne's strategy. Larvitar leads with Smooth Rock to extend sandstorm to 8 turns, then sets Stealth Rock before the player can stop it. Geodude with Wide Lens makes Rollout 99% accurate — the snowball almost never misses. Nosepass is the ace: Sand Force boosts Power Gem by 1.3x in sand, Thunder Wave paralyzes the player's best counter, and Leftovers provides passive healing. In sandstorm, Nosepass's 90 SpDef becomes 135 effective — combined with 135 Def, it's a fortress. The player must solve sand + paralysis + hazards + Rollout before fighting Roxanne.

## Design Notes
- Marc is the final gym trainer gauntlet before Roxanne — the hardest non-Leader fight in Segment 1
- Larvitar with Smooth Rock extends sandstorm from 5 to 8 turns, matching Roxanne's own lead
- Wide Lens Geodude is the same setup Roxanne uses — the player learns to respect 99% accuracy Rollout
- Nosepass at L13 with Sand Force + Power Gem is 90 SpA x 1.3 = 117 effective SpA in sand
- Thunder Wave on Nosepass teaches the player about paralysis — slowed and turn-skipping is devastating
- Leftovers on Nosepass means it recovers HP each turn while the player is paralyzed
- This fight directly prepares the player for Roxanne: sand + hazards + items + Rollout + special Rock STAB
- The player who beats Marc understands every mechanic in Roxanne's team

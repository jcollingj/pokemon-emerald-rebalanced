## Puzzle
James is the Dustox-path showcase and plays completely differently from Lyle. Nincada opens by stacking Sand Attack accuracy drops while Speed Boost builds — the player watches it get faster each turn. Dustox is the star: Prankster Poison Powder poisons with +1 priority, then Venoshock deals doubled damage (130 BP with STAB = 195 effective) against the poisoned target. Protect stalls for poison chip. The player must either KO Dustox fast or bring a Poison/Steel type that resists Poison Powder.

## Design Notes
- James is the "Dustox path" twin to Lyle's "Beautifly path"
- Nincada with Speed Boost previews the Ninjask evolution line — the player sees speed ramping
- Dustox's Prankster + Poison Powder + Venoshock combo is a 2-step condition-payoff pattern
- Surskit adds Bug/Water coverage and Swift Swim hints at rain synergy
- The contrast between Lyle (Tinted Lens offense) and James (Prankster status control) teaches the player that bugs come in offensive and defensive flavors
- Nincada and Surskit are both from the Petalburg Woods encounter pool

# Sarah (Lady) — Route 116

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Skitty | 10 | None | Normalize | Fake Out, Sing, Tackle, Growl |
| Ralts | 10 | None | Trace | Confusion, Disarming Voice, Calm Mind, Growl |
| Eevee | 10 | None | Adaptability | Swift, Wish, Tackle, Growl |

## Puzzle
Sarah runs the "rare and elegant" team. Skitty opens with Normalize Fake Out for a guaranteed flinch, then Sing threatens sleep — the player has to survive two turns of disruption before they can fight back. Ralts uses Calm Mind to boost and fires off Psychic/Fairy dual STAB. Eevee is the rarest wild encounter on Route 116 (1% rate), and Adaptability Swift hits at 120 effective BP (never misses) — a Normal-type nuke the player might not expect. Wish lets Eevee heal itself or pass healing to teammates.

## Design Notes
- Sarah is the Lady trainer, so she uses "pretty" and rare Pokemon — Skitty, Ralts, Eevee
- Eevee appearing on a trainer previews it for the player — they might not know it's catchable on Route 116
- Adaptability Swift at 120 effective BP is genuinely strong at this level — the player sees Eevee's potential
- Ralts with Calm Mind at L10 continues to teach the player about setup sweepers
- Skitty's Sing + Fake Out is a two-turn disruption combo: flinch turn 1, sleep turn 2
- No Nugget — Sarah is a Lady who fights with style, not money

# Nina (Aroma Lady) — Trick House Room 3: The Poison Room

## Lesson
**You need status cures — the clock is ticking.** Three sources of Toxic/Poison spread across three mons. The longer the fight goes, the more passive damage accumulates.

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Tentacool | 22 | Toxic Orb | Poison Heal | Toxic, Whirlpool, Scald, Wrap |
| Dustox | 22 | Black Sludge | Shield Dust | Toxic, Protect, Moonblast, Psybeam |
| Roselia | 22 | Big Root | Triage | Leech Seed, Giga Drain, Toxic, Venoshock |

## Puzzle

Nina's Tentacool opens with Whirlpool to trap, then Toxic. It has Toxic Orb + Poison Heal — it isn't scared of the player's status, and it heals while sitting in Toxic Orb. The player is trapped and ticking. Dustox comes out and Toxics the next Pokemon, then Protects to waste another poison/leech turn. Shield Dust prevents any secondary effects from landing on Dustox. Finally, Roselia leads with Triage priority Leech Seed (goes before any non-priority move) then priority Giga Drain every turn — healing itself while dealing damage. The player's remaining Pokemon are toxic'd, seeded, and taking passive damage from all sides. The puzzle: bring Antidotes/Full Heals, or a Poison/Steel type (immune to Toxic), or blow through quickly before the chip adds up.

## Design Notes
- Tentacool's Toxic Orb + Poison Heal is a recurring Aqua Grunt design element (from the museum fight). Nina mirrors this mechanic, teaching the player that Poison Heal Tentacool cannot be status-stalled.
- Dustox's Shield Dust blocks secondary effects — Scald's 30% burn chance, Thunder Wave paralysis chance, etc. It tanks hits without worrying about secondary status. The player can't burn-stall it.
- Protect on Dustox wastes one of the player's turns per cycle — each Protect forces an extra turn of Toxic ticking.
- Roselia with Triage: priority Leech Seed lands before the player's next move, guaranteeing the seed gets in even against faster Pokemon. Then priority Giga Drain hits back and heals — the player is simultaneously draining their HP and having it drained from Leech Seed.
- Big Root on Roselia boosts Giga Drain's healing by 30%, making it very hard to outlast.
- Venoshock on Roselia: if the player's Pokemon is already poisoned (likely from Tentacool or Dustox), Venoshock doubles to 130 BP + STAB = 195 effective. This is the fight's finisher.

# Dock (Sailor) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Carvanha | 21 | None | Strong Jaw | Crunch, Aqua Jet, Ice Fang, Scary Face |
| Poochyena | 22 | Flame Orb | Guts | Crunch, Facade, Fire Fang, Howl |
| Tentacool | 21 | Oran Berry | Poison Heal | Scald, Whirlpool, Toxic, Wrap |

## Puzzle

Dock's team is built around a trap for status users. Carvanha hits hard with Strong Jaw Crunch and Ice Fang from turn 1 — fast and fragile. When it goes down, Poochyena enters with Flame Orb + Guts. Here's the trap: if the player tries to burn Poochyena with Will-O-Wisp or use any fire-type status strategy, Guts is ALREADY active from the Flame Orb, and the burn overwrite does nothing new. Poochyena's Facade hits for 140 BP at Guts-boosted Attack. Howl adds even more. Tentacool closes with Whirlpool trap + Toxic stall. The puzzle: you can't status Poochyena safely. You need raw power or a resist.

## Design Notes
- Poochyena with Flame Orb is the Guts lesson: self-inflicted status for permanent 1.5× Attack boost. The burn halves Attack for everything *except* Guts Pokemon, which turn that burn into fuel.
- Facade at 70 BP doubles to 140 when statused — Guts Facade is a 140 × 1.5 = 210 effective BP Normal move. This is the player's wake-up call about Guts.
- Carvanha teaches the player to remove fast glass cannons immediately. Strong Jaw Crunch has 1.5× power — 80 × 1.5 = 120 effective BP off Carvanha's decent Attack.
- Tentacool as a finisher: Dock is a tough sailor — his closer is a trapping Poison Heal wall to test remaining resources.
- Scary Face on Carvanha is key: it slows the next Pokemon coming in, which may make Poochyena's Guts turn more dangerous if the player can't hit back quickly.

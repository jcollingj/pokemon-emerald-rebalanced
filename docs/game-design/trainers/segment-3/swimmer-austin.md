# Austin (Swimmer M) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Horsea | 22 | Scope Lens | Sniper | Snipe Shot, Smokescreen, Focus Energy, Bubble Beam |
| Carvanha | 22 | Life Orb | Strong Jaw | Crunch, Aqua Jet, Bite, Scary Face |

## Puzzle

Austin puts the player in a no-win offensive trap. Horsea opens with Smokescreen (accuracy -1 stage) then fires Snipe Shot: Sniper's passive +1 crit stage + Snipe Shot's built-in +1 + Scope Lens's +1 = +3 crit stages = **guaranteed crits every hit** for 2.25x damage. The player can't rely on bulk because every Snipe Shot crits. Focus Energy is available if Smokescreen was skipped — still guarantees crits. Switching in a bulky wall just invites Carvanha: Aqua Jet priority (40 BP × 1.5 STAB = 60 effective, ignoring bulk entirely), Crunch at 80 BP × 1.5 Strong Jaw × 1.5 STAB = 270 effective BP. Scary Face cripples the player's speedsters. The player can't turtle vs Horsea (crits bypass bulk math) and can't outrun Carvanha (priority). Austin forces the player to go on offense.

## Design Notes
- The switching trap: staying in vs Horsea means accuracy drops + guaranteed crits. Switching means eating Carvanha's Aqua Jet priority on the way in.
- Horsea's Scope Lens is the unlock item — it pushes Snipe Shot to guaranteed crits via Sniper's passive +1 + move's +1 + Scope Lens +1 = +3
- Smokescreen first is important: accuracy -1 before guaranteed crits means the player's counterattacks also miss 1 in 4 times, extending Horsea's life
- Life Orb on Carvanha: recoil doesn't matter when Aqua Jet is threatening to KO anything already chipped by Horsea
- Scary Face cripples speed-reliant Pokemon that might otherwise outpace Carvanha's Aqua Jet follow-up with their own priority
- The lesson: passive/defensive strategies fail against the crit + priority combination. Offense is the answer.
- Counter: a bulky Electric or Psychic type that can take Aqua Jet + Crunch damage and retaliate before Horsea can set up more Smokescreen stacks

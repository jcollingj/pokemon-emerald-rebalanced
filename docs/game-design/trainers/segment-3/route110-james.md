# James (Cyclist M) — Route 110

## Team

| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Poochyena | 20 | None | Guts | Bite, Take Down, Howl, Scary Face |
| Gulpin | 21 | Sitrus Berry | Gluttony | Toxic, Yawn, Encore, Sludge |

## Puzzle

James is a status layer fight — two Pokemon that each apply different forms of pressure, forcing the player to deal with overlapping threats. He's on the main route, not the Cycling Road, so he's accessible to the full party.

Poochyena leads with Scary Face (Speed −2) to slow down the player's fastest Pokemon, then Howl (+1 Attack) and starts clicking Bite/Take Down. A slowed Pokemon can't revenge-kill Poochyena as easily, and Guts means if someone tries to burn or paralyze it to reduce damage, it gets a 1.5× Attack boost instead. Bite's 30% flinch stacks with the speed drop for chip turns.

When Poochyena is low, Gulpin switches in and starts the status clock: Toxic begins counting, Yawn threatens sleep on the next turn (forcing the player to switch out or eat the sleep), and Encore locks anything that used a non-attacking move into repeating it. Gluttony fires the Sitrus Berry at 50% HP instead of 25%, giving Gulpin a little extra staying power. Sludge is Gulpin's actual damage threat — Poison STAB at L21.

## Design Notes

- James is the accessible Route 110 trainer for players who skip the Cycling Road. He's easier than the Triathletes but introduces the status layering concept.
- Poochyena's Scary Face is the opener — slowing the player's lead means Gulpin's Yawn has time to land before the player can KO it.
- Gulpin can't do the Belly Drum combo yet (evolves at L26, gets Belly Drum on evolution) — but it can already threaten status and light damage. Its presence foreshadows the Swalot that players might raise.
- Encore is specifically designed to punish players who use healing items or pivot moves during the fight. James rewards aggressive play and punishes cautious play.
- The Guts + status combo on Poochyena is a preview/reminder of the Guts mechanic — the player has probably seen it on Segment 1-2 trainers and should know not to use Will-O-Wisp or Toxic on it.

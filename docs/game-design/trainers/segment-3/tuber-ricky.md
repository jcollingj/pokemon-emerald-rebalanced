# Ricky (Tuber M) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Marill | 21 | Sitrus Berry | Huge Power | Aqua Jet, Play Rough, Defense Curl, Rollout |
| Wailmer | 22 | Leftovers | Blubber | Water Spout, Body Slam, Amnesia, Dive |

## Puzzle

Ricky is the "don't judge a Pokemon by its sprite" trainer. Marill looks like a harmless blue ball but Huge Power **doubles its Attack stat** — suddenly Aqua Jet hits for 40 BP × 2 (Huge Power) × 1.5 (STAB) = 120 effective priority, and Play Rough hits for 90 BP × 2 × 1.5 = 270 effective. Marill opens with Defense Curl (Defense +1, doubles Rollout's power) then Rollout for escalating damage, or just priority Aqua Jet to punish switches. Wailmer follows as the second lesson: this cute round whale fires Water Spout at **150 effective BP from full HP**. Blubber heals 1/8 HP per turn, Leftovers heals another 1/16 — Wailmer recovers 2/8 HP/turn, meaning it stays near full HP and keeps Water Spout's damage window open. Amnesia doubles SpAtk if Wailmer gets a free turn.

## Design Notes
- Ricky is a Tuber (kid trainer) — his Pokemon should look cute and harmless but genuinely threaten
- The core lesson: read the ability, not the species appearance. Marill's 30 Atk becomes 60 with Huge Power — the dex entry "it has a real small attack" is an outright lie
- Sitrus Berry on Marill: heals after the first hit it takes, giving it one more Aqua Jet/Play Rough attempt. A bulky hit-and-run attacker.
- Wailmer with Blubber (1/8 HP/turn) + Leftovers (1/16 HP/turn) = 3/16 HP per turn. After taking a 40% hit, Wailmer regenerates back into Water Spout's peak damage zone in 2 turns.
- Dive is the safety valve: if Wailmer is about to be KO'd, it goes invulnerable for one turn and heals 3/16 HP in the process, coming back into Water Spout range.
- Counter: a fast Electric-type OHKOs Wailmer before it can Dive, and Grass-type hits Marill's Fairy typing weakness isn't present (Water/Fairy takes neutral from Grass). Actually Azumarill is weak to Grass/Electric. The lesson for the player is attack early and fast.
- Escalation: Marill (deceptive physical power) → Wailmer (deceptive bulk + nuke) — both teach the same lesson but from different angles

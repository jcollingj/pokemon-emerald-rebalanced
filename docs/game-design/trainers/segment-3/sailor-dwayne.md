# Dwayne (Sailor) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Carvanha | 22 | Choice Band | Strong Jaw | Crunch, Aqua Jet, Psychic Fangs, Bite |
| Tentacool | 22 | Toxic Orb | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Acid Spray, Scald, Knock Off |
| Gyarados | 23 | Lum Berry | Moxie | Waterfall, Dragon Dance, Crunch, Ice Fang |

## Puzzle

Dwayne's team is a three-wave escalation. Wave 1: Carvanha with Choice Band. Crunch = 80 × 1.5 (Strong Jaw) × 1.5 (STAB) × 1.5 (Band) = 270 effective BP. Aqua Jet = 40 × 1.5 (STAB) × 1.5 (Band) = 90 effective BP priority. Psychic Fangs (Strong Jaw boosted) covers Fighting/Poison types that resist Dark/Water. The player must KO Carvanha fast. Wave 2: Tentacool comes in to mop up whatever survived Carvanha. Toxic Orb activates Poison Heal. Whirlpool traps the player's attacker. Acid Spray -2 SpDef, Scald burns. Knock Off strips the player's item (removing their recovery, Choice Scarf, etc.). Wave 3: Gyarados closes. Lum Berry ensures one Dragon Dance goes through cleanly. After +1 Atk/Spe, the first KO triggers Moxie for +2 Atk total. Nothing survives the sweep.

## Design Notes
- Dwayne is a sailor — his team feels like the sea itself. Waves of aggression, then entrapment, then a rampaging leviathan.
- Carvanha + Choice Band is the test: "can you handle a glass cannon that hits absurdly hard?" The answer is to outspeed and OHKO before it fires. At 75 Speed on Carvanha, it outspeeds most L22 Pokemon.
- Tentacool's Knock Off is the critical move against the player: strips Lum Berry (removing status protection from Gyarados counters), Eviolite, or Life Orb from the player's key attacker.
- Gyarados with Lum Berry specifically covers the "freeze Gyarados before Dragon Dance" counter. It gets one free DD. That's all it needs.
- Dragon Dance + Moxie: after DD, Gyarados is at 86×1.5 = 129 Speed (outspeeds most), 125×1.5 = 187 Atk. First KO = +2 Atk (188 base, then +1 from Moxie on top of DD). Waterfall at 220+ effective BP off 250 Atk clears teams.
- Ice Fang covers Grass-types that resist Water. Crunch covers Psychic/Ghost answers.
- Counter: a fast Electric OHKO on Carvanha, then a status-spreader (Toxic, Paralyze) on Gyarados before Dragon Dance. Dwayne only loses to players with type coverage AND speed.

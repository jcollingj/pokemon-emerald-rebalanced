# Aqua Grunt 1 — Slateport Oceanic Museum

## Team

| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|

## Puzzle

**Theme: Fast and Hungry.** Grunt 1 opens Team Aqua's museum presence with base-form Pokemon that still bite hard. Each slot applies a different kind of pressure — speed, trap, setup, and brute force — without any held items to make it manageable. The player needs to identify threats before they snowball.

- **Carvanha (lead):** Strong Jaw Crunch off 90 Atk hits way harder than it looks at L20. Scary Face punishes players who try to speed control. Aqua Jet picks off weakened Pokemon attempting to chip. This is a glass cannon on 20/20 defenses — it dies to almost anything, but threatens to KO a fast Pokemon or revenge something weakened. The player learns: "kill it fast or it kills something fast."

- **Tentacool (pivot):** Whirlpool traps a Pokemon and lowers its Attack, then Acid Spray guarantees -2 SpDef for follow-up Scald. Even without a Toxic Orb, Tentacool's 100 SpDef means it survives long enough to run the trap loop. The player is introduced to the Whirlpool + Acid Spray shell — a preview of what Tentacruel will do in Grunt 2's team, but without the healing to make it truly oppressive.

- **Poochyena (disruptor):** Guts + Swagger is the trick. If Swagger confuses the player's Pokemon and they hit themselves, Poochyena's Guts Bite off 85 Atk starts chunking. Howl boosts Attack before Swagger to ensure Bite hurts whether or not confusion triggers. Quick Attack is the safety valve — anything Poochyena can't finish, it can priority-finish. The puzzle: "do I risk eating a Swagger and hitting myself, or spend turns to remove it?"

- **Corphish (closer):** Sheer Force Crunch (Dark STAB, flinch → damage boost = 117 effective) and Aqua Jet priority create a do-or-die matchup. Leer cuts Defense first to soften the target. At L22, Corphish is close to matching an evolved player Pokemon in raw pressure. Vise Grip is the fallback for Normal coverage. The player has had 3 Pokemon to chip it down — if they failed to manage resources, Corphish punishes.

## Design Notes

- No held items on this grunt — they're a grunt, not a boss. The team's puzzle is mechanical (trap, confusion, priority) rather than item-enhanced.
- Carvanha's main design is Strong Jaw STAB: Crunch at L20 (80 BP × 1.5 Strong Jaw × 1.5 STAB = 180 effective). It genuinely threatens fast or frail player Pokemon.
- Tentacool's Acid Spray (−2 SpDef) previews the trap-tank loop without the Toxic Orb payoff — player sees the pieces but doesn't face the full engine until Grunt 2.
- Poochyena's Swagger targets whatever the player sends in. Against bulky Normal/Dark-resistant options, Howl + Guts Bite still hits hard. Against fragile glass cannons, the confusion timer is enough.
- Corphish's Sheer Force interacts with Crunch's flinch effect (suppressed, converted to +30% damage) — the player won't see the flinch happen, but Crunch will feel stronger than expected.
- Escalation within the fight: fast glass cannon → trapper → confusion disruptor → brute. Each Pokemon runs a different win condition, keeping the player off-balance.

# Lola (Tuber F) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Corsola | 21 | Leftovers | Regenerator | Stealth Rock, Scald, Flip Turn, Recover |
| Azumarill | 22 | Sitrus Berry | Huge Power | Belly Drum, Aqua Jet, Play Rough, Brick Break |

## Puzzle

Lola runs a four-step setup sequence the player must interrupt at one of three points. Step 1: Corsola sets Stealth Rock. Step 2: Corsola uses Flip Turn — attacks for chip damage AND pivots to Azumarill, triggering Regenerator (heals 33% HP on switch-out). Step 3: Azumarill immediately Belly Drums — costs 50% HP, grants +6 Attack, Sitrus Berry heals 25% back. Azumarill is now at 75% HP with 660 effective Attack (Huge Power doubles base 55 Atk, then +6 stages). Step 4: Aqua Jet priority sweeps the team. The three interruption windows: (1) KO Corsola before Stealth Rock lands, (2) Taunt Corsola before Flip Turn, or (3) OHKO Azumarill before Belly Drum. Missing all three windows is almost certainly a loss.

## Design Notes
- Lola being a child trainer (Tuber) makes this extra humiliating — getting swept by a kid with a scripted 4-step sequence
- The sequence is intentionally obvious and telegraphed. A player who knows the design will disrupt it; a player who doesn't will get swept and learn.
- Corsola's Flip Turn is crucial: it prevents safe Taunting on the switch (Flip Turn attacks first, deals damage, THEN Azumarill comes in). By the time Azumarill is in, it's the player's turn to respond.
- Azumarill at +6 Atk with 660 effective Attack + priority Aqua Jet hits through almost any defensive Pokemon. Play Rough covers anything that resists Water.
- Stealth Rock makes switching out to find an answer cost 12.5% HP each time
- Counter: Priority moves that OHKO before Belly Drum (a fast Thunderbolt from Electrike, for example), or a Grass-type that resists Aqua Jet and threatens Azumarill with 4x Grass damage
- Escalation: support → safe pivot → setup → nuke. Four clean steps. The lesson: identify and break setup chains before they complete.

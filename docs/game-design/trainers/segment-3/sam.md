# Sam (Tuber M) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Wailmer | 21 | None | Water Veil | Surf, Rollout, Growl, Water Pulse |
| Marill | 20 | Sitrus Berry | Huge Power | Aqua Jet, Belly Drum, Defense Curl, Rollout |

## Puzzle

Sam's tiny tuber looks harmless. Wailmer opens and just uses Surf and Rollout — it's not threatening, it's big and slow. But then Marill comes in with Huge Power and the real threat: Belly Drum to max Attack, then Sitrus Berry fires immediately (at 50% HP threshold). After setup, Aqua Jet has +1 priority — the player cannot outspeed it. Defense Curl + Rollout is a secondary threat if Belly Drum isn't used. The puzzle: if you can't OHKO Marill before it drums, you have to eat an Aqua Jet at +6 effective Attack. Bring a special attacker or a Pokemon with enough bulk to survive priority and counter.

## Design Notes
- Marill without Gluttony still uses Sitrus Berry at 25% threshold, but Belly Drum drops to exactly 50% HP — the Berry fires immediately. This is how vanilla Belly Drum + Sitrus works on Marill.
- Huge Power doubles Attack (vanilla) — so Marill's 20 base Attack becomes 40 effective. After Belly Drum: 160 effective Attack on a Pokemon whose base stat is 20. At level 20, that's genuinely threatening.
- Rollout on Wailmer is a preview: if Wailmer uses Defense Curl + Rollout, it doubles each hit. By turn 5, it's doing 480 BP. This telegraphs the same mechanic that Marill uses.
- The tuber trainer class (small child on a floaty) makes this feel like a funny surprise — a little kid has a genuinely dangerous Belly Drum Marill.

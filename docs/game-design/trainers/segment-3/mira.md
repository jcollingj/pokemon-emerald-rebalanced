# Mira (Psychic) — Trick House Room 2: The Lock Room

## Lesson
**You need diverse movesets — one move gets locked.** Mira's team cycles through Disable → Encore → Prankster Taunt, eventually shutting down every single-move specialist.

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Ralts | 21 | None | Synchronize | Disable, Psybeam, Growl, Confusion |
| Sableye | 22 | Leftovers | Prankster | Will-O-Wisp, Taunt, Feint Attack, Shadow Sneak |
| Whismur | 21 | None | Soundproof | Encore, Screech, Echoed Voice, Uproar |

## Puzzle

Mira's team locks moves in sequence. Ralts uses Disable on the first strong move the player fires — that move is unavailable for 4 turns. Then Sableye enters with Prankster Taunt (+1 priority) — the player can't use any non-attacking moves (so no heals, no setup, no status). Prankster Will-O-Wisp burns physical attackers before they can move. Whismur's Encore then locks the player into the one attacking move they're left with after Disable expires. By the end, the player is burning, locked into one move, and being Screech'd to death. The puzzle: run at least 2 different attacking moves. One can be Disabled, but the other keeps you flexible.

## Design Notes
- Ralts's Synchronize reflects any status Mira inflicts on herself back at the player — but the design doesn't lean into that here. It's flavor.
- Sableye's Prankster chain is the core lock: Will-O-Wisp first (before physical attackers move), then Taunt to prevent recovery or healing items from being used. This forces the player to "fight through" with whatever they have.
- Whismur's Soundproof makes it immune to all sound-based moves — Screech from another Pokemon won't affect Whismur itself, so Mira is confident using it.
- Echoed Voice stacks — each consecutive use doubles in power (100 BP on the second use, 200 on the third). If Whismur Encores the player into a non-damaging move, it has free Echoed Voice turns while they're locked.
- Uproar is Whismur's panic button: 90 BP Normal move that prevents anyone from sleeping for 3 turns. If the player is trying to use sleep moves, Uproar shuts it down.

# Tyler (Swimmer M) — Route 109

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Wingull | 20 | None | Keen Eye | Wing Attack, U-turn, Growl, Water Gun |
| Tentacool | 21 | Oran Berry | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Toxic, Acid Spray, Scald |

## Puzzle

Tyler opens with Wingull using U-turn — it chips, then immediately swaps to Tentacool. Tentacool traps with Whirlpool first, then stacks Toxic while the player is stuck. Acid Spray drops SpDef to make Scald sting harder. The puzzle: kill Wingull before it can U-turn, or bring something that resists both Poison (for Toxic) and Water (for Scald) to deal with the trap cleanly.

## Design Notes
- Wingull's U-turn into Tentacool is the simplest form of pivot + trap — the player's intro to "kill the pivot before it switches"
- Poison Heal on Tentacool: Toxic Orb would be the player's tell that Tentacool isn't scared of status — but Tyler has no Toxic Orb, so Toxic on Tentacool is just passive chip, not the strategy a Poison Heal Tentacool would normally run. This Tentacool uses Toxic offensively.
- Whirlpool trapping while Toxic ticks is the lesson: you can't switch out of a bad matchup if you're trapped. Bring a Poison-immune Pokemon (Steel, Poison types) or a Ground/Electric type to clean Tentacool quickly.

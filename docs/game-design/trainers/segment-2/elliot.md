# Uelliot (Fisherman) — Route 106

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Tentacool | 16 | Oran Berry | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Acid Spray, Water Gun, Poison Sting |
| Wailmer | 17 | Sitrus Berry | Blubber | Water Pulse, Aqua Ring, Surf |
| Magikarp | 18 | None | Moxie | Tackle, Aqua Jet, Flail |

## Puzzle
Elliot runs the "wear you down then ambush you" strategy. Tentacool opens with Whirlpool to trap and chip, using Acid Spray to strip SpDef and Poison Sting for poison chance — the player's lead Pokemon takes sustained damage with no escape. Wailmer is the frustrating middle act: Blubber heals 1/8 HP every turn, Aqua Ring stacks another 1/16, and Sitrus Berry gives a free HP burst when low — it takes sustained, consistent damage to kill it before it recovers. By the time Wailmer falls, the player's team is drained. Then Magikarp appears. The trap: players instinctively see Magikarp as free cleanup, but a weakened player Pokemon trying to chip it down may die to Flail (100–150 BP at low HP) before finishing the job. If Magikarp gets any KO, Moxie boosts its Attack and Aqua Jet becomes a dangerous priority sweeper. The counter is to save a full-HP Pokemon specifically for Magikarp, then OHKO it immediately.

## Design Notes
- Magikarp's rebalanced stats (50 Atk, 40 HP) make it a functional Pokemon — Moxie + Aqua Jet with real Attack is threatening priority, not a joke
- Flail power: 100 BP at 1/4 HP, 150 BP at 1/8 HP — a weakened Magikarp getting attacked by a weakened player Pokemon can flip the script
- Wailmer's Blubber + Aqua Ring + Sitrus Berry creates a mini sustain loop: heals 1/8 + 1/16 per turn, plus ~40+ HP burst from Sitrus. Effectively impossible to whittle down without 2 consecutive solid hits
- The Tentacool → Wailmer → Magikarp sequence tells a story: trap you, grind you, ambush you
- This trainer rewards players who learned "Magikarp is dangerous now" from Darian on Route 116
- Different from Douglas (who uses rain synergy) and Kyla (who uses Staryu beams) — Elliot is purely about resource drain and the Magikarp gut-punch

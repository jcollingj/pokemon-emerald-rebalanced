# Beverly (Swimmer F) — Route 105

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Wingull | 16 | None | Drizzle | Supersonic, Water Pulse, Wing Attack, Growl |
| Tentacool | 17 | Oran Berry | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Acid Spray, Scald, Poison Sting |
| Wailmer | 17 | None | Blubber | Water Pulse, Aqua Ring, Dive, Water Gun |

## Puzzle
Beverly stacks confusion from multiple angles: Wingull fires Supersonic (guaranteed confusion if it hits) then Water Pulse (20% confusion), Wailmer continues with another Water Pulse. A confused player Pokemon hitting itself while Tentacool sets Whirlpool + Acid Spray is a full-team lockdown. Growl + Acid Spray + Scald burns creates a four-debuff pile-up that grinds down even durable Pokemon.

**The solve:** Be aggressive — don't let confusion land. KO Wingull before Supersonic goes off (it's the confusion anchor). Once Wingull is down, the confusion chain collapses. A fast Electric or Grass attacker that outspeeds all three can clean through before the lockdown establishes. Avoid switching: Whirlpool traps whatever it catches, and the Whirlpool + Acid Spray + Scald sequence is devastating on anything stuck in.

## Design Notes
- Beverly is the "chaos trainer" — her team relies on hitting the player with overlapping status sources before they can respond
- Differentiated from Luis (speed+trap) and Dominik (rain damage) by focusing purely on confusion stacking and stat drops
- Three confusion sources (Supersonic, Water Pulse ×2) means the player will likely be confused at some point — the lesson is to attack before confusion is applied
- Wailmer's Blubber + Aqua Ring makes it surprisingly durable even at L17: Aqua Ring restores HP during Dive's invulnerable turn, stalling for another confusion attempt
- Oran Berry on Tentacool lets it take a hit before trapping — Tentacool's 100 SpDef means it survives almost any special hit anyway, so the Berry keeps it healthy against physical priority moves

# Luis (Swimmer M) — Route 105

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Carvanha | 17 | None | Strong Jaw | Scary Face, Crunch, Aqua Jet, Bite |
| Tentacool | 18 | Oran Berry | Poison Heal | Whirlpool, Acid Spray, Scald, Poison Sting |

## Puzzle
Luis is a two-Pokemon pressure test. Carvanha opens with Scary Face to cut the player's speed by two stages, then bites hard with Strong Jaw Crunch (120 eff BP) or Aqua Jet for priority if the player tries to switch. Tentacool immediately traps the weakened Pokemon with Whirlpool (trap + Atk -1), Acid Spray for a guaranteed -2 SpDef drop, then Scald to burn what's left. The Oran Berry keeps Tentacool healthy through early chip damage.

**The solve:** KO Carvanha before Scary Face goes off — it has only 45 HP and 20 Def, so a strong hit deletes it. Then handle Tentacool with a Ground-type move or super-effective coverage before it sets up the trap-and-spray combo. Don't let both moves land: Acid Spray −2 SpDef + Scald burn makes any Pokemon crumble.

## Design Notes
- Luis is the "introduction trainer" on Route 105 — teaches the player that ocean Pokemon bite hard and trap
- Carvanha at L17 has its full opening combo: Scary Face (speed control) + Crunch (Strong Jaw STAB) + Aqua Jet (priority)
- Tentacool's Whirlpool + Acid Spray combination is the signature threat of the route's encounter pool — Luis showcases it early
- Two-Pokemon team keeps the fight short and focused — a clean appetizer before the route's bigger challenges
- Oran Berry on Tentacool lets it survive one hit before establishing the trap

# Foster (Ruin Maniac) — Route 105

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Omanyte | 17 | White Herb | Swift Swim | Shell Smash, Water Gun, Ancient Power, Bite |
| Anorith | 18 | Eviolite | Battle Armor | X-Scissor, Rock Blast, Aqua Jet, Metal Claw |
| Lileep | 17 | Big Root | Suction Cups | Giga Drain, Ancient Power, Confuse Ray, Ingrain |

## Puzzle
Foster's fossil team has three distinct threats that demand three different answers. Omanyte leads with Shell Smash: White Herb negates the −Def/−SpDef drops, leaving a +2 Atk/SpA/Spe Omanyte firing STAB Water Gun and Ancient Power. Anorith has Battle Armor (no crits — the player can't luck into a kill), priority Aqua Jet, and Eviolite bulk. Lileep is functionally immovable: Ingrain prevents voluntary switching out while Suction Cups blocks forced switches — it can only be KO'd, not removed. Giga Drain + Big Root heals for enormous amounts per use.

**The solve:** Preempt Omanyte's Shell Smash — it's fragile at +0, and a fast Grass or Electric move deletes it before setup. Against Anorith: don't rely on crits through Battle Armor; it has priority Aqua Jet so don't expect to underspeed it safely. Against Lileep: this is a pure damage race — Giga Drain + Big Root returns major HP each use, so use Fire, Ice, or strong coverage to burn through before healing catches up. Chipping with weak moves is futile.

## Design Notes
- Foster uses the three fossil Pokemon from Roxanne's team — thematic for a Ruin Maniac who recovered specimens from the same ancient sites
- Shell Smash + White Herb on Omanyte mirrors Roxanne's team strategy (the player has seen this before) — a call-back that rewards attentive players
- Battle Armor on Anorith is a direct reminder that crits aren't always available; one of the few Pokemon in the game that specifically punishes RNG-dependent offensive strategies
- Lileep's Suction Cups + Ingrain combo is a "you must deal with this directly" puzzle: Whirlpool won't eject it, Dragon Tail won't work, Roar won't work — the player must KO it
- Big Root on Lileep amplifies Giga Drain to ~150% return per use, making any slow chip game losing for the player

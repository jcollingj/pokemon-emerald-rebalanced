# Josue (Bird Keeper) — Route 105

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Taillow | 16 | None | Guts | Aerial Ace, Quick Attack, Growl, Peck |
| Wingull | 17 | Damp Rock | Drizzle | Water Pulse, Wing Attack, Supersonic, Roost |
| Pelipper | 19 | Sitrus Berry | Drizzle | Scald, U-Turn, Knock Off, Roost |

## Puzzle
Josue's team punishes the two most common counter-strategies on this route. Taillow leads: Aerial Ace never misses and Guts makes it STRONGER if the player uses burn or paralysis — status moves backfire here. Wingull sets Drizzle (Damp Rock = 8 turns) and fires rain-boosted Water Pulses with confusion chance while Roosting to stay healthy. Pelipper resets Drizzle on entry — the rain never expires. Scald in rain (120 eff BP + 30% burn), U-Turn to refresh rain, Knock Off to strip held items.

**The solve:** Don't use status moves on Taillow (Guts makes it stronger when burned/paralyzed). Electric types shred Wingull (4x) and Pelipper (2x) — a strong Electric attacker ends the rain chain immediately. Without rain, Pelipper becomes a slow bird with no offensive identity. Knock Off will strip Sitrus/Oran Berries, so don't rely on mid-battle healing to survive Josue's team.

## Design Notes
- Josue is the "anti-synergy" trainer: the two most natural counter-strategies (status, hitting water birds with Electric) don't cleanly solve the whole team
- Taillow's Guts specifically punishes burn from Scald (the player's best status move on this route) — the player must hit it directly or switch
- The Drizzle double-up (both Wingull and Pelipper carry Drizzle) means the rain refreshes on every pivot — the rain is functionally permanent unless the player uses weather override
- Pelipper's moves (Scald, U-Turn, Knock Off, Roost) are from its full designed build even at L19, consistent with trainer team conventions in this game
- Knock Off is specifically designed to punish players who relied on Oran/Sitrus Berry healing through Beverly and Imani's fights earlier on the route

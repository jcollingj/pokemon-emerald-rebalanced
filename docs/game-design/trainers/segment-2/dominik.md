# Dominik (Swimmer M) — Route 105

## Team
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Wingull | 16 | Damp Rock | Drizzle | Water Pulse, Wing Attack, Supersonic, Roost |
| Wailmer | 18 | Sitrus Berry | Blubber | Water Pulse, Aqua Ring, Dive, Water Gun |
| Carvanha | 17 | None | Strong Jaw | Crunch, Aqua Jet, Bite, Scary Face |

## Puzzle
Dominik's puzzle is rain management. Wingull leads and Drizzle activates immediately — Damp Rock extends rain to 8 turns. Water Pulse in rain hits at 90 effective BP with 20% confusion, and the weather window is long. Wailmer tanks chip damage with Blubber + Sitrus Berry + Aqua Ring healing while firing rain-boosted Water Pulses. Carvanha closes with Aqua Jet priority and Strong Jaw Crunch.

**The solve:** Kill Wingull first. It has Roost to recover, so hit hard before it heals (Electric is 4x). Once rain expires, Wailmer and Carvanha lose their weather boost. Electric handles Wingull and Carvanha; Grass handles Wailmer. Don't let 8 full rain turns pass — confusion from Water Pulse + Supersonic adds up fast.

## Design Notes
- Dominik introduces Drizzle/rain as a team mechanic, previewing what Josue (Bird Keeper) executes at a higher level
- Three Pokemon gives the player a complete rain team experience: setter, bulky abuser, closer
- Wailmer's Blubber + Aqua Ring creates frustrating sustain: 1/8 per turn (Blubber) + 1/16 per turn (Aqua Ring) + Sitrus Berry burst heal — the player needs to finish it quickly or it becomes a heal loop
- Carvanha as the closer rewards players who cleared the rain quickly but still face a speed/priority threat
- No item on Carvanha keeps it glass cannon — satisfying to OHKO if the player correctly identified the threat order

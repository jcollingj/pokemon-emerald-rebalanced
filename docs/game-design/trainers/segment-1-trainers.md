# Segment 1 Trainers: Start to Rustboro Gym

## Design Rules
- All trainers have **3-6 Pokemon**
- Pokemon should be from the **local area** (what the player can catch nearby)
- A few surprise picks are fine, but most should be representative
- Every trainer is a **mini-puzzle** — competitive movesets, not filler
- Trainers should use Pokemon at appropriate levels for their route

### Daisy (Aroma Lady) — Optional
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Shroomish | 13 | Miracle Seed | Technician | Bullet Seed, Mach Punch, Stun Spore, Absorb |
| Lotad | 13 | Leftovers | Rain Dish | Absorb, Leech Seed, Rain Dance, Mega Drain |
| Roselia | 14 | Big Root | Triage | Absorb, Mega Drain, Poison Sting, Growth |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** The healing loop. Roselia drains with priority Triage moves before the player can respond — every Mega Drain goes first and heals Roselia back. Lotad sets up Rain Dance (activating Rain Dish healing) then stalls with Leech Seed. Shroomish punishes slow or fragile teams with priority Mach Punch. The player needs Fire/Flying/Ice to exploit Grass weaknesses, but needs to work fast before the sustain snowballs out of control. Mach Punch punishes fragile Fire/Flying leads that can't take a hit.

### Miguel (Pokefan) — Optional
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|

| Marill | 15 | Sitrus Berry | Huge Power | Water Gun, Aqua Jet, Play Rough, Defense Curl |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** Miguel's Pokefan team has a hidden chain. Skitty leads with Fake Out flinch on turn 1, then Attract to infatuate. Wingull switches in and sets Drizzle rain (8 turns with Sitrus sustain + Supersonic confusion). Zigzagoon threatens a Belly Drum setup sweep — Pickup's Multiscale-at-full-HP lets it survive a hit to set up. Marill is the closer: Huge Power Aqua Jet is boosted by rain and hits with +1 priority. Player must choose: KO Wingull first to shut down rain (then Zigzagoon is the bigger threat), or KO Zigzagoon to stop the sweep (then rain-boosted Aqua Jet follows).

### Andrew (Fisherman) — Optional
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Magikarp | 13 | Lum Berry | Moxie | Tackle, Flail |
| Tentacool | 13 | Black Sludge | Poison Heal | Poison Sting, Water Gun, Whirlpool, Acid Spray |
| Poliwag | 14 | Damp Rock | Swift Swim | Hypnosis, Pound, Rain Dance, Water Pulse |
| Magikarp | 15 | (none) | Moxie | Tackle, Flail, Aqua Jet |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** The Fisherman's Gambit. The first Magikarp looks pathetic but Flail hits extremely hard at low HP — and Lum Berry blocks status before it can go off. Tentacool traps with Whirlpool (can't switch out) then Acid Spray drops SpDef by 2 stages so everything hurts more. Poliwag puts something to sleep with Hypnosis, then sets Rain Dance for Swift Swim speed doubling + rain-boosted Water Pulse spam. Final Magikarp has Aqua Jet priority and is scarier than it looks. The misdirection is the point: the humble Magikarp is the actual threat.

### Amy & Liv (Twins) — Optional, Double Battle

**Amy's team:**
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Plusle | 15 | Oran Berry | Plus | Thundershock, Quick Attack, Helping Hand, Nuzzle |
| Squirtle | 14 | Leftovers | Mega Launcher | Water Gun, Water Pulse, Aura Sphere, Rapid Spin |

**Liv's team:**
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Minun | 15 | Oran Berry | Minus | Thundershock, Quick Attack, Helping Hand, Charm |
| Marill | 14 | Sitrus Berry | Huge Power | Water Gun, Aqua Jet, Play Rough, Defense Curl |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** The Plus/Minus doubles combo. When Plusle (Plus) and Minun (Minus) are both on the field, each has their SpA boosted by 1.5×. They use Helping Hand to amplify each other's Thundershock — or boost Squirtle's Water Pulse for massive damage. Nuzzle (Plusle) paralyzes a target; Charm (Minun) drops the player's physical attacker's Attack by 2 stages. Squirtle's Mega Launcher makes Water Pulse hit everything for boosted power. Marill threatens priority Aqua Jet to clean up weakened targets. To win: KO Plusle or Minun immediately to break the Plus/Minus SpA boost before Helping Hand + boosted Thundershock cleans up.

### Marcos (Guitarist) — Optional
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Voltorb | 13 | Magnet | Static | Thundershock, Charge, Sonicboom, Rollout |
| Pikachu | 14 | Light Ball | Transistor | Thundershock, Quick Attack, Nuzzle, Double Team |
| Voltorb | 15 | Sitrus Berry | Static | Thundershock, Charge, Sonicboom, Selfdestruct |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** Static paralysis + glass cannon threat. Touching either Voltorb with a contact move risks Static paralysis. Pikachu with Light Ball + Transistor has 180 effective SpA on Electric moves — every Thundershock at L14 hits far harder than expected. Nuzzle paralyzes a target for guaranteed speed reduction. The second Voltorb threatens Selfdestruct for massive Normal damage when low on HP. Player needs Ground-type moves for Voltorbs, but then Quick Attack from Pikachu threatens fragile Ground types. Don't use Electric against this team. Don't use contact moves unless you can afford paralysis. The puzzle is resource management around Static.

### Rhett (Black Belt) — Optional
| Pokemon | Level | Item | Ability | Moves |
|---------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Makuhita | 14 | Sitrus Berry | Guts | Fake Out, Arm Thrust, Vital Throw, Focus Energy |
| Shroomish | 14 | Miracle Seed | Technician | Bullet Seed, Mach Punch, Stun Spore, Mega Drain |
| Zigzagoon | 15 | (none) | Pickup | Quick Attack, Headbutt, Belly Drum, Pin Missile |
| Poliwag | 16 | Damp Rock | Swift Swim | Hypnosis, Rain Dance, Water Pulse, Mud Shot |

**Status:** designed
**Mini-puzzle:** The hardest optional fight on Route 103 — each Pokemon punishes a different reactive strategy. Makuhita has Guts: burning or poisoning it with Scald/Toxic makes it hit 1.5× harder. Players who try the "burn the fighter" reflex get punished hard. Fake Out flinches turn 1. Focus Energy + Vital Throw = guaranteed-hit critical hit Fighting attacks. Shroomish has priority Mach Punch (Technician) and multi-hit Bullet Seed to break through defensive play. Zigzagoon can Belly Drum + Quick Attack sweep — Pickup's Multiscale-at-full-HP means it survives a hit to set up. Poliwag Hypnosis sleeps your best attacker, then Rain Dance + Swift Swim doubles speed for a closing Water Pulse sweep. Solution: Don't status Makuhita — KO it directly with Psychic or Flying. Use priority to interrupt Belly Drum. Bring fast attackers that can OHKO before setup. Rhett teaches advanced counterplay at every step.
